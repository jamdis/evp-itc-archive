Hi LC and Stefan.  I think the term skeptic is often misapplied to those who are cynics.  
Skepticism is healthy and usually these people are somewhat open-minded given enough 
persuasive evidence, whereas someone who is cynical is not open to changing their view 
no matter how much evidence is presented them.  

Just a thought that semantics might be getting in the way here, with some people using 
the word skeptic in it traditional/literal sense of the word, and other are actually meaning 
"cynic" but using the term skeptic.
