My dealings with Findhorn (in Scotland) have helped immensely in my

called Devas, Faeries, and Elementals? Nope. That's just what they

Do I combine my Spiritualism with my EVP/ITC research? Nope. Not

answering my questions in real time. Or simply answer them with no  
radio at all - just a faucet trickling or no sound whatsoever.

What about others here? Do you combine your beliefs with your
