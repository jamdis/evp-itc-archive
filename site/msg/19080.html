Frank,

I never announce that I am doing a session, they just seem to know when I am
ready.  I agree with you wholly that they are ready to transmit.  Multiple
ITC researchers have intimated that they have transmission stations on the
other side.  This was even documented by Raudive's EVP's 40 years ago.  I am
quite convinced that they do gather and a technician wrangles them.  I have
numerous clips of a technician reiterating my words to the Others and I also
have numerous clips of a technician telling them to some up and talk.

I wish I could see how they have it set-up over there.  What baffles me is
why they need equipment to contact us, let alone how their equipment is
built and used.  It seems as if their world is a physical one just like
ours.  Macy reported this years ago and I dismissed it then, but now I am
slowly reconsidering what he has said.  But I have yet to get a clear answer
on what their environment is like when I ask or how they can talk to us.
 The closest I got was "bending energy."
