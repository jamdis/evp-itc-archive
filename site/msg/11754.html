The 469 uses a very low powered amplifier circuit on the headphone jack.  Stand computer speakers will not really work for it and you will get a very low audio out of it if anything.  What you need is a small set of amplified speakers.  Radio Shack sells a few seats that fold up for around $20.00.  I got mine when they were on sale for $10.00.  Or any good amplified speakers will do the job for you.  But remember the clocking on am.  The clicking will be amplified too so try and ignore it if possible.
