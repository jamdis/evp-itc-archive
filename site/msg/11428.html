Angela,

If you are looking to get started with a simple ghost box, i would 
suggest a 20-125 model by radio shack.  It incorporates a speaker and 
is able to sweep AM, FM and shortwave.  The simple procedure is on my 
site.

http://www.keyportparanormal.com/simpleghostboxhack.html

Other models that are available for the hack are as follows:

12-469 (New Model White)---Pull the Mute pin off the board once 
opened.

20-125 --- Cut wire on ribbon going to the "Mute" markings on the 
board.

12-820 --- Cut wire going to "Mute" on the board

12-150 --- Cut Wire going to "Mute" on the board.  This is my latest 
find and is a bit harder to get at the wiring, but can be done.  It 
also incorporates a built in speaker.

12-469 and 12-820 will need an external speaker, but they come with 
earbuds.  I use a 21-549 speaker from radio shack, it works well and 
is fairly inexpensive.

-Steve H.
