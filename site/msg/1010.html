Just to add to what others said -

If you're talking about speakers on your computer, it quite often is a
driver problem as well.  I have a Soundblaster X-FI (with the front
panel I/O) for example, and the drivers are notoriously finicky under
XP.  The last time it went out, it was just like you described - loud
pop through the speakers then no sound.  I build my own PC's and work
in software, so troubleshooting is down my alley and I proceeded to
troubleshoot after that happened.  Reseated the card, uninstalled and
reinstalled the drivers, etc. etc.  Wouldn't work right until several
weeks later after another reboot everything finally was mapped right.
