By the way, I meant to these two questions a while ago:

1) With your random station switching circuit, how long is the
interval between switches?

2) What's the algorithm for the random switching?  Is there anything
in the circuit that cuts out the possibility of it staying within
close proximity of the previous tuning interval?  For example software
wise if I were generating a random number, unless I have a true random
number generation sometimes the "randomness" follows predictable
patterns, even recalling the same number over and over.  So for
example if your circuit is doing a change every millisecond but could
possibly return to the same station several times during a 10
millisecond period, the result would be the possibility of a single
station being tuned for the majority of that 10 millisecond period
rather than the truly random tunings you're looking for.
