Yes, the speaker is about a foot from the lamp/phototransistor combo. Volume has to be adjusted so the mic does not overload, it is a touchy adjustment of audio and flame level. If the flame is set too high the unit generates lots of noise from the flutter of the flame. Also the room should be dark and computer monitor turned down to reduce noise the mic will pick up produced stray light sources.
