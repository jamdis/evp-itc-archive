Sorry to hear about the difficulty, Robin. What was the feedback rating of 
the seller? I have never had problems with any eBay seller whose feedback 
was 98% positive or better.

Water (except for distilled water) contains mineral salts that are 
electrically conductive. When it dries it leaves behind these salts as 
residues which are also conductive. You might be able to clean the phone 
if you get to it fast enough, but sometimes the conductivity of water puts 
voltages on parts where it doesn't belong and ruins them. I'd guess the 
phone is a goner.
