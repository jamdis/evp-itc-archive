Hi magnolia.massage,

I started more than a year ago with a radio shack "hacked" box that
another member hacked for me so that it continuously scanned stations.
Here's what I did: despite feeling a little silly, I turned on the
radio and my Olympus digital recorder, and I asked my questions in the
direction of the radio (it felt like I was talking to myself, and I
expected nothing to happen). After 5 minutes or so, I quit recording
then uploaded the .wav file to Sound Forge on my computer. It sounded
pretty much like hearing myself talk to a scanning radio -- nothing
intelligible there. Then I used Sound Forge to slow down the gibberish
I heard in the scanning portions, and as I experimented with slower
playback speeds I began hearing "Hi Al" and all sorts of other
comments that could not possibly have been a part of radio programming
fragments. As I mentioned in my first post the other day, this
eventually turned into a distressing situation for me in which the
radio shack radio began turning itself on, literally, each morning and
I later began hearing the voices without aid of a radio. I assure you
I am telling you the 100 percent truth.  Apparently, something
"negative" attached itself here and it took much effort and help from
a Catholic priest to get rid of it. I don't exactly know what we did
that was THE solution to get rid of it, but it finally left.  I knew
nothing about how to protect myself at that time. Although the
negative energies may not be able to physically harm us (I still
wonder about that), the emotional/mental distress can be every bit as
bad or dangerous as physical harm. I've begun slowly experimenting
again but this time I am trying some new techniques to protect myself.
I've been told by experienced ghost-box users that if your intent is
positive, you will attract positive voices, and that is the theory I'm
using now as I cautiously pursue the hobby again.

My only advice would be that, if you do proceed, to use caution and to
remember that the voices lie. If I were you, I would not take any
action based on what a voice says without checking it out in the usual
human ways first.

If you're interested in hearing more about my previous experience,
just drop me a private note off list.

Regards,
Al
