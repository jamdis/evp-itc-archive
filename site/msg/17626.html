Hard to get a perfect sweep, because it's constantly changing. A sweep may be
good here at say setting #6 with 30 stations. But your sweep may be better at
#8 with only 20 stations. Not to mention atmospheric conditions and whether you
are inside or outside.

If you think that would work well for you, just mark the spot with a dab of
white out. If the condition changes, you can always scratch it right off.
