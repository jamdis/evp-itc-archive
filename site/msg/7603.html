There is the "Black Hole antenna" invented by John F. Sutton of NASA.

they work amazingly well.  They are broadbanded over several decades. You 
just have to build your own and then get the hang of tuning them 
initially ... no one sells one of these beasts and many people don't evne 
believe they will work.
