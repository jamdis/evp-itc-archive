A ghost in the darkness I am
Creeping silently through the shadows.
Desperate not to be heard.
I appear in lucid form.
It is time at last.
I wanted to be heard.

You stare at me in silence.
Confused but now aware.
"Why are you here?"
You ask in a voice filled with dread.
I glance at you,
A gentle smile upon my face.
"I am here to teach all that I have learned."

You stare at me in silence.
Confused but now aware.
"What are you?"
You ask in a voice filled with awe.
I glance at you
A gentle smile upon my face.
"A gray Witch and Hybrid Vamp I am,
a piece of your own soul."

Your mouth opens, no words you do speak.
Astonishment fills your being.
As I speak these words of mine
As I becalm you, Oh student of mine.
I speak words of wisdom
Directly from your heart to mine.
"Remember my child, the truth shall set you free."
