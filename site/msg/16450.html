I find her attitude irritating. Yes, anything can be faked. A photo, an
audio recording, a driver's license can all be faked. But does that mean
that ALL photos, audio recordings and driver's licenses are fakes? A person
who accuses others of misdeeds is often found guilty of the same misdeeds.
Maybe we'd better have a look at her driver's license.

I can often hear things in recordings that others can't. It comes from years
as a radio amateur trying to hear extremely weak signals in extremely noisy
backgrounds. This includes EME (Earth-Moon-Earth) which can be some of the
most difficult to hear. I heard things in some recordings I made at a
haunted place and sent them to some other people. They couldn't hear them.

Having said that, I'm not a fan of "orbs." I spent a lot of time exploring
how to get photos of "orbs" with a digital camera with flash. I'd say 99.9%
of the orb photos are due to natural causes. There IS that 0.1% I can't
explain, though.
