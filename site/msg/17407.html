I typically refrain from posting on the boards precisely because of people like you who feel that their insipid comments are worth hearing.  There is nothing wrong with skepticism, hell, I have been in a relationship with a laboratory scientist who is an atheist for going on four years now so I know what skepticism is supposed to sound like.  I hear it everyday.  Your posts do not reflect the nature of skepticism, they reflect outright denial.  There is a difference betwixt the two, and it is not slight.

Why?  Because you claim to want irrefutable proof.  Yet, you do nothing about it.  As the old adage goes, it's time for you to sh*t or get off the pot.  Get your hands on a ghost box and start experimenting.  There are numerous radios to hack yourself, so do it already (granted, a hack is by no means up to par with a Frank's Box, Minibox, or any of the other guys doing this, but it will get you started).  Then take the time to design controlled experiments to be implemented.  Share your results.  Write up a paper about it.  Follow the scientific method.  There are ghost box users all over the country, so I am sure you can find someone relatively close to you to work with if you actually want to take the time and responsibility of adding any knowledge to this community. 

Do something, anything, other than gripe about not having enough 'proof.'  Epistemological knowledge has been debated by much better minds for thousands of years and the very notion of proof is still, well, unprovable.  Exactly like science.  What we believe today may not be true next year.  Talk to any post-modernist in this post-modern society we live in and he will tell you that the notion of scientific proof is meaningless precisely because its very knowledge base is in constant flux.  Gravity may be here today as a constant, but we cannot know with certainty the same will be true tomorrow.  This is why we have the scientific method to explore variables.  This is why skepticism is good. 

We are all individuals, functioning individually in this field.  We do not have government funding.  We do not have PhD's working on this stuff.  We have guys and girls, in their homes and shops, trying to understand the incomprehensible.  Can you not realize that we all know that the evidence we collect is instantly disprovable and will always be so?  Just the simple fact of audio clips being posted here abnegates the power of empiricism.  As you have stated, you are a skeptic.  No audio clip will provide you with empirical evidence because you did not collect it yourself and therefore too many variables involved make it easily dismissable with just an iota of scientific rigor.  We accept that, and you cannot.  Therefore, your mere presence alone on these forums is antagonistic to the work we do because we can never provide you with proof.

How can you expect proof when we do not even know with certainty whether we are talking to spirits, ET's, gods, or frickin unicorns.

How can you expect proof when the very nature of spirit and afterlife is wholly unprovable.

How can you expect proof in a yahoo forum?  Does not the very nature of a forum defy all skeptical enquiry?

And finally, speaking of forums, you are a guest in one run by people who believe this to be true expressly for people who believe likewise.  

So why are you here?

So please, do something to add to the community and quit relying on others to do it for you.  If you keep relying on this forum to give you proof, you will always be disappointed because there is no proof other than the audio clips you deny.  

Period.

For the other forum users, I'm sorry to feed the troll this time.  I know its best to let them starve, but once in a while you just might be able to shoo them away from the bridge to allow a safe crossing (yes, pun intended).
