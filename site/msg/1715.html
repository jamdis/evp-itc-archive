Yep, more often than than not. The echo box seems to be almost the number 
one factor in how clear the voices are. All of these tonight have been with 
one of the small wave guide type boxes. Sizes shown on the diagram are only 
approximate, I tend to make them in "what feels right" sizes. They can be 
made out of wood, metal, or plastic, maybe card board(?), same as the 
channel seperators. It does need to be air tight! In the one I used tonight 
the top and bottom were 1/16th thick aluminum, and the sides, ends, and 
seprators 1/8 inch lexan, (plexiglass). The speaker is from a modem, about 
one inch in diameter, and the mic a 3/8th diameter electret.
