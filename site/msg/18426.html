Once upon a time the cordless phones and baby monitors both operated in the
49 MHz part of the spectrum. It wasn't unusual to be able to hear baby
monitors on cordless phones.

I also had a piece of ham radio equipment for the 6-meter band (50 MHz) that
would tune below that. I would listen to the phone conversations on the
cordless phones and also the baby monitors once in a while. But it got
boring fast!
