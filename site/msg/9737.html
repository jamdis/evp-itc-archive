1.Open sound soap

2.top menu bar click FILE, on the drop down menu click on
"open media file"

allaphone file and double click the file.

4.Set all features on the sound soap control panel to the "off"
position. There are are two up and down slide bars, left side is
for "REMOVE CLICK AND CRACKLE" the right side is for "ENHANCE" both
OFF. There are two round dials a right and left, make sure they are
slid all the way "COUNTER CLOCKWISE"

left button is for returning the file to the beggining. The right
button with the arrow is the play button. On the right side of the
play bar there is a button labeled apply. do not touch it.

7.To use the program and do a session, click on the play button, when
the file begins to play click the moving slider arrow on the bottom
of the play bar and hold down your left mouse button. Thios allows
you to "DRAG THE SLIDER ARROW BACK AND FORTH ALONG THE PLAY BAR"

This will facilitae manually scrambling the order the file plays in
which allows our spirit friends to manipulate the tones into words.
