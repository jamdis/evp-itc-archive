on one megahertz, i had 2 old zenith transoceanics which would give 
out a persistent but weak one kilohertz tone when local 0ne meg 
station was nulled out and flywheel rocked slowly between 900-1100 khz

after awhile myriad weak voices would appear next to the 1000hz tone, 
which is the peak in hearing sensitivity of most humans AND the 
SQUARE ROOT of one megahertz which seems to imply a weak 
superregeneration with quench as square root of carrier.  this 
happens on no othe frequency only one meg.  it would be hard to 
automate because the min and max is random and determined by hearing 
the click of the spurious superregeneration kicking in.

on one occasion in 1986 i was fooling around with this and abruptly 
got full rendition of a 78 rpm version of judy garland singing over 
the rainbow and it was LOUD and extra extra clear.  i had time to run 
outside and conscript my wife as a witness before it switched off 
with a few indecipherable male voices and a click
