guessed at least a half dozen different voices were gotten on EVP. Some
male, some female. All gave direct answers to our questions that made sense
as answers to the questions, even if wrong (e.g. "Do you know what year it
is?" - EVP: "1996." The actual year was 2006.) One of the funniest was in
answer to the question "Do you want us to leave?" simultaneously a male
voice said "Yes" and a female voice said "No." So there definitely seemed to
be some interaction between us and whatever was answering the questions.

the EVP there might have been residual. It was hard to say, as we were the
first known group to ever investigate the building. But I got a name "I am
(pause) Francey (pause) William." I did some searching with Google and found
there was a Francey family in that town once, although I coundn't come up
with any records for someone named William Francey. Someone else is digging
into that.

It's all very interesting, no matter what.
