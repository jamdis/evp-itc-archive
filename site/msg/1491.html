Thanks Steve, I calculate the audio sample rate to be between 20-100ms tuning manually. Auto tuning would produce the same results and is less fatiguing on the operator. Any method that can produce clean audio samples at this rate should work, I have tried a few different ways, the radio tuning being the best so far. As with any method 'intention' seems to be a factor in getting any results. The linear tuning of a radio is a true random method. The content of each broadcast channel is always changing, so the audio samples are always different sweeping back and forth across the spectrum. Heres a couple more from the 7/8/07 session.
