worked on the principle of mutual induction or inductive transfer.

there is a magnetic induction field which radiates the audio signal 
from the telephone receiver.

pickup devices' and the energy (current changes, etc.) is transferred 
down the wire and into a device that can record or play (audio) what 
is being picked up by the 'telephone pickup device'.

Box in their ad is a complete false bit of advestising.
The 'Frank's Box' as we all know does not work under this principle.

What the advertised device appears to do, or attempts to do, is 
search for magnetic fields and produces some kind of sound when it 
finds them...???  The association with Frank's Box is for advertising
