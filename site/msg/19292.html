John Cage was an avant-garde music composer/producer. The Imaginary
Landscape #4 was a recording of 24 men and 12 radios, two men on each
radio. One got to rotate the tuning knob randomly according to his
whim, and the other rotated the volume knob.

Not the most bizarre piece he produced.
