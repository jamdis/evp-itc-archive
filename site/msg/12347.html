Hi Suzanne,

The Ovilus and Frank's Box are two completely different animals, so-
to-speak.
One is a series of circuits that cause a radio frequency to sweep
using a method of "white noise" to stimulate that sweep pattern,
while the other is a series of digital circuits using a number of
sensors(I'm guessing)to stimulate speech and cascading LEDs to
perform based on what mode it's in.

My box was thrown together as a vacation hobby, with more added as I
found more room to add... and as I found interesting devices on
paranormal websites.
I did scribble some schematics down as I went, and I guess I should
try to gather those up and put together some sort of diagram.

Let me work on that... in my spare time. :-)

everyone here.
