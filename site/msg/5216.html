Yes Frank, I saw Juli's initial suggestion and several people agreed
it should be done, but I didn't see a real start date. If we set some
basic parameters that can be followed it may yield noteworthy results.

 There was a huge study that went on for a few years that was done by
Alexander MacRae that included unlabeled evps. Average length of time
of an evp, average # of words, etc. There were hundreds of
participants world wide including Guy Jackson who is a participant in
this forum. I meet Mr. MacRae at the AA-EVP conference a few years
back and found his work to be fascinating. -Bill
