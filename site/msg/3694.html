I pulled this list of general recommendations for The MiniBox right 
off Ron's www.paranormalsystems.com page -

1. Do not allow children under the age of 16 to use this device 

2. Although individual sessions are effective, group sessions are more 
productive.

3. This is not an 'electronic Ouija board' - no harm can come from 
using it. 

4. Some voices will use coarse language so be prepared for it. 

5. Always use a recorder to tape all sessions 

6. Keep a notebook with the times and locations of your sessions. 

7. The MiniBox will call you by name so don't be startled by it.
