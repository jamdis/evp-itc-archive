Hey David, Don't give up, yet.  I just got back to read all the posts, mostly dedicated to you.  I actually had written a few responses after my first one to you but was not a able to complete my thoughts so it went onto "draft." 
 
I did take time to look at you web site and I am impressed.  You obviously have excellent scientific/engineering credentials to warrant saying "I want you on my side."  If some of the messages we are receiving are from other than traditional EVP sources, I want to know about them.  Alien, hoaxer, CIA, MIB, Anunnaki, or whatever.  As well, it would be great to know about frequencies, QM, electromagnetic readings, everything and anything that you could add to the data would be terrific...at some point in time.  But, using Remote viewing as an example, which you investigated and discussed on your web sit, don't you think the "application" of this phenomena (I won't yet elevate it to the term "technology") is worth serious investigation.  Remote viewing was developed due to the results it got.  It never was, and still has not been investigated and accepted by the "scientific community."  In other words it has not been subjected standards of proof.  Yet it works.  I know this because I have proved it to myself.  I know EVP works because I have proved it to myself.
 
So, my question is from, let's call it, a "marketing approach."  That is my back group, when I was in the world of making money for a living.   My questioning process went something like this:  1) what does it do   2) what benefit does it have to me and others   3) what can I do with it.   With this approach we developed cable TV and satellite services....I was selling cable tv and 24 hour news channels when people were asking "why in the world would you need more than three channels ---  abc, nbc and cbs?   And, who in the world would want a 24 hour news channel?  Then I was involved in selling car navigation systems in the 80's and portable cell phones that weighed and were shaped like a brick and sold for $5,000.  What's that got to do with EVP-ITC?  It has to do with the people involved EVP-ITC...we are at the forefront.   Some of us have touched the other side and know what is there.  We do not need to prove it exists to anyone else...although acknowledgment from others at times would be nice and certainly appreciated. 
 
In my first email to you I suggested that you get a radio shack hack and go talk to it.  Maybe you have already done a zillion hours with one, but I am not aware of any posts you have done.   If possible can you direct me to where I can listen to your actual recordings?  I did not find them here or on your web site.  Also, I have a few EVP messages of my own that I would really like you to hear and analyze in any way you can and I will send them to you.  Let's do this in private emails, if you will. 
 
And finally, let me just add to the application aspect of this work.  I know of a number of EVPers who have been working with law enforcement to help with missing persons and murder cases.  If you have not visited Sandra Belanger's web site at www.heliumraven.com I would encourage you to do so and let me know your thoughts.   I would also like to learn how we can get information from the other side to help with medical problems like autism in children and to help solve our energy issues.  If we get answers that are usable from the other side in law enforcement, to learn about relatives who have passed on, or to solve medical or other real world problems, does it really matter where or how the messages got here?   I is where the value is in this and other groups who do not use the scientific method.  I can't speak for everyone here but I think we should welcome someone like you to work with us.  keep up the good work   ron l
