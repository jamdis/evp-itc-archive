Sometimes they can be complicated. The FM sweep tops out at about 10.5
volts, while the AM is 7.5 volts. So either I have to lose part of the FM
band, and only sweep to 7.5 volts, have dead air in parts of AM, or do some
level switching. I'm running two sweep modes, random and linear, 4 sweep
levels, Random AM and FM, and Linear AM and FM. So I have to incorporate the
sweep mode and band switch together. A 4 pole double throw rotary switch for
band and a SPDT switch from sweep mode. Plus a SPDT switch for speaker
mode==echo chamber or direct. Then power is DPDT switch, which is part of
the tone control.

 It's kinda fun to think this crap up.
