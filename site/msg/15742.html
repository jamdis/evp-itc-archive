Frank  I am not addressing your abilities or you specifically, just this prediction, and how people are now seeing it after August 15th has passed.  I know you are the first people to admit this stuff can't be proved, and often, you can't even trust what you hear.

Out of all the predictions that seemed associated with August 15 over the past month or two, and hints about comets, and meteors, and big rocks or other things hitting the ocean, and the earth being doomed, and the end of time: out of all these and more, to pull out "the coasts are clobbered  the eastern one", during hurricane season, and say that one was accurate, I see as problematic.  As I said, in the case of people who try to predict the future, to ignore all the rest and imply that they were successful on one unremarkable element in a very large prediction, doesn't really serve them well.   

I think the best way to show that EVPs are provable would be with a prediction.  But, that is also the best way to discredit them.  So if anyone does decide to take the path of proving these things, I would recommend gathering and publishing the predictions they hear (before the event happens, of course).  If someone does not want to take the path of trying to prove these things, they I suggest they stay away from publishing predictions (or trying to pick and choose their pieces), as that seems to only cause stress, confusion, or worse, when the events do not occur.

Just my opinion.  

And please don't reply with "Bite Me" again Frank.  I'd hate for you to have to ban yourself from your own group. :-)
