Yes, Steve...I agree.  The top board is all that needs to be lifted.  
I became too aggressive and had the whole dang thing tore apart. I 
just lifted the board up and identified the 7th wire from the right 
matching up with the MU on the bottom board (needed a flashlight to 
see real good) and pushed my Xacto hobby knife through the wire.  By 
the way, if the SW button up top is not in properly when re-
assembling the radio, it can prevent the radio from functioning if it 
is stuck in the "on" position.  The button is not needed if you lose 
it down inside the radio.
