I have one of a handful of the Edison Autobiography that has the last chapter with his thoughts on the great beyond.  His family had that version of the biography recalled and reissued it without the last chapter.

Anyway in this last chapter, Edison alludes to a way to communicate with the dead but he never says he did it.  He simply hypothesizes on how it could be done and the fact that the dead are just on a different wavelength than the living.

Sorry, no links...  This is from that old fashioned thing called a book :))
