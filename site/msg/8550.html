Well, there are a lot of explanations for this, but none of them has
been proven yet (afaik). I tend to the "synchronicity" explanation - at
least when pure logical devices such as computers are used, which I
believe can't be influenced mentally, neither by spirits nor by humans
(otherwise they would probably crash more often than already without
such a manipulation). ;)

I think that causal models aren't sufficient to explain EVP. If no
devices are influenced directly, there must be taking place some other
form of "manipulation". Perhaps the "probability field" is manipulated
somehow in cases where random number generators (RNG) are involved? As
we know from psychokinesis experiments in parapsychological labs, test
persons were able to get "hits" even when "prerecorded targets" are used
instead of a RNG. It seems that time and causality doesn't matter in
respect to paranormal phenomena.
