A lot of the DR60's have been selling in the $200-300 range on eBay, though as Steve 
pointed out... many still pay $500.  I agree that the quantity of EVP is very good with this 
unit, but the quality doesn't compare to that of very reasonably priced units (I'm talking 
under $50) like the Sony B500/B26/B16/B7/ST25, Panasonic US360, etc.  

I guess it's all about a personal preference between quantity vs quality, and of course for 
those of us who have a slew a recorders, adding one more to the arsenal.  LOL.  

I think I might be one of the luckiest people on the planet.  I got my DR60 on a Buy-It-
Now for a total of $39 ($29 + $10 shipping).  

And I agree about box research being a lot more productive and clear.  I like all my units.  
But, I also like working with traditional EVP as well.  I have to have variety!  ;-)

Margaret
