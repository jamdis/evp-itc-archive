Frank Sumption wrote:
Hi All;

This is how I make the "echo box". The frame is 1/4 inch poplar from Lowes,
the foil in the heavy duty aluminum foil, and it is glued on with contact
cement. You can see the mic and speaker mounted opposite each other on wood
sides. The sides are 1/8" plywood expoxied into the frame. It gives a solid
mounting panel for the mic and speaker, and can be card board, as well. I
try to avoid plasitc, as it seems to be "contra-ghost voice" if that makes
any sense.
