discussing. I got the name Nicholson Lawson a few sessions ago. I  
asked if that was one person's full name or two people's last names,  
and was told it was one. I went through records at Ancestry.com and  
found a Nicholson Lawson who was listed as a carpenter in the  
Louisville, Kentucky 1890 directory, and what may be the same man in  
New Haven, Connecticut in 1919. These are the only two records of  
anyone by that name in the U.S. or U.K. that are currently indexed.  
It might not be the same Nicholson Lawson, but its such an unusual  
name. I didn't expect to find anyone in the historic record with that  
name, honestly, but there he is.
