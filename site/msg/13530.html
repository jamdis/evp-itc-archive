I do not have an ovilus or a puck - I was going to get one, but I participated in many sessions alternating puck, ovilus, and radio devices.

 

Although at times we would get relevant responses on the puck and ovilus, more often than not it would be confusing, irrelevant, and disjointed.


We would invariably return to the the radios and have much better results, so at this time I have decided to concentrate my efforts on radios and a couple of other methods and areas I am still researching.

 

I always encourage people to do their own research, though. If you want to, go for it!

Just let us know what happens.
The results I talked about were not meant to discourage you, just to tell you what we experienced. Maybe some people do better with the puck and ovilus. Not everyone is meant to do everything.
