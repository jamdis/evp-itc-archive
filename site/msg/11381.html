Hi Guy,

> There is NO research anywhere which shows that germanium diodes are
> superior to silicon diodes as far as the "diode method" is concerned.

Maybe there's no such research, but germanium diodes are preferably used
for demodulating AM signals because of their lower forward voltage drop of
0,3 volts (silicon have 0,7 V). This means, germanium diodes can detect
waeker signals. http://www.reuk.co.uk/Germanium-Diodes.htm
Of course, also Shottky diodes are very well suited; their forward
voltage drop is even lower than for germanium diodes.
