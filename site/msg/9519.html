Hello Rhonda and Everyone,
 
I have heard my name called out loud before, but it was not an EVP situation it was after my first girlfriend died. I was 17. She was 15. She had had her second kidney transplant a few months earlier and died of gas gangrene.
 
She died at 3:15 PM and I felt it. I knew that she had died. It is very difficult to explain, but I knew that she was gone. So, it wasn't surprising to me when I got home from school and my eldest sister was on the phone telling my Mother that Rena had died.
 
My Mother had moved us to Texas from Oklahoma a few months earlier. I knew that Rena wasn't doing good as she was before I left, but no one expected her to die. She made a turn for the worse late that morning and by 3:15 she was gone.
 
Late that afternoon I took the Greyhound bus back to my hometown in Oklahoma. When I got to my sisters house, it was around 1 am. My sister is 18 years older than me, so I have a niece that is a year younger than me. My niece and I both heard Rena call my name and I then heard her call my name again a little later before I went to bed. That night after I had gone to sleep, it woke me up as I felt someone sit on the side of my bed. I knew it was Rena telling me she was okay, but this was before I knew anything about the paranormal, so I was a bit scared to open my eyes and see her.
 
She was my first love and I am hoping that I will hear from her someday via EVP/ITC.
 
I consider myself very blessed to of heard her call my name and sit beside me to let me know she was okay.
 
Wishing everyone a wonderful weekend.
