I'm not sure.

This concept has its rabid detractors and -- like so many studies -- it's in the early, wild-and-crazy "what if...?" phase.  

All the numbers show is that an RNG (random number generator) anomaly occurred. There's speculation that the anomalies relate to global consciousness.  I'm not convinced that's the whole story.

For now, scientists are taking RNG anomalies and -- once the anomalies are clear and have concluded -- data-mining backwards to associate them with world events.

It's interesting what events seem linked to RNG anomalies and which events seem not to affect them.  The numbers turned anomalous hours before Princess Diana's death, the 9/11 attacks, and the Asian tsunami.

However, other apparently important events -- and some may argue, more important events -- don't seem to connect to RNG anomalies.

Other RNG anomalies can't be linked to one event, but may relate to several.

For example, the RNG sequences were anomalous at the time of the volcano eruption in Iceland. However, the timing also coincided with the Kumbh Mela in India, and the big earthquake in Qinghai China.  Did one, two or all of them relate to the RNG variation... or were the RNG anomalies coincidental?  (Rhetorical question.)  

So, I look at the May 7th RNG numbers and I see an anomaly. I'm not aware of anything in our world that correlates to that date.

This may be piling wild extrapolation on top of wild extrapolation, but I speculate that these RNG anomalies aren't just about our world.  They may reflect something larger.

Or, the RNG anomalies may be a reaction to something far more mundane, but not yet measurable or detectable. 

At this point, it's too early to predict anything in June.  I think I'll be watching the numbers starting about a week before the 17th.

This is the kind of quirky study that intrigues me.  It may amount to nothing, but my "gut feeling" is: Something useful is in those RNG studies.  I just don't know what it is, yet.
