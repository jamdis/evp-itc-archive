Chad, I went to Big Lots and got a cheap looking portable set of
speakers. It was about $7 but it was probably the most portable they
had. It is an all-in-one speaker set. The speaker is small, white, and
oval shaped. It has both the left and right speaker and plugs directly
into the radio and just sits on top of it. It has a little power-on
switch on top and is powered by two AAA batteries. The brand name of
it is "Kinyo," which I have never heard of. Even though it was a no
name brand as far as I could tell, it gave me the results that I had
in the cemetery and in my opinion it's the ultimate in portability
that I have seen.
