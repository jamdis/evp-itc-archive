Many, maybe most of these "digital" radios are actually analog tuners that
are voltage tuned. So instead of removing the tuner, as these are large
boards, just add a sweep board. It's the same thing Hultay does. It has the
advange of much better sound, since you're using he amplifiers and speakers
that it was designed with. This also has the built in tape deck.

 The radio is kind of a crap shoot, as some are all digital, and the tuner
is tuned with software, usually IC-to-IC(ICsquared) a language put out by
Philips. Some car tuners use this, and I even had a couple Arm Band radio
that are all digital tuning.
