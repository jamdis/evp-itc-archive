Just dug out the manual for the Icom R-75 shortwave receiver that has 
been in box for 2 years since I moved.  It has programable scan start 
and stop frequencies, and variable scan steps and speed, along with 
various modes - USB/LSB/RTTY/CW/AM/FM - AND programable wide and 
narrow filters.  All of these settings, along with AGC, ANL and other 
functions can be changed while the radio is scanning.  Not very 
portable and rather expensive, but it is here.  I've got 2 projects 
ahead of starting to fool around with this, but I imagine it could be 
useful in finding frequencies/modes that work.  Ideas?
