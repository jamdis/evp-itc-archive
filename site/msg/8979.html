Hi Kay.  I'm guessing the ones buying these units feel that having it come 'ready to go' is 
worth it for one reason or another.  Which makes sense.  They are paying for both a good 
and a service.  Some people might not have the tools at hand, and don't want to have to 
borrow from others.  Some people are doing all their experiments in secrecy, and do not 
want friends or loved ones to know what they are doing.  In that case, having a unit arrive 
pre-hacked would be very convenient.  

This is SO completely off topic, but are you old enough to remember "Pet Rocks?" (mid-
70's)  Talking about the radios made me have the thought that people will buy most 
anything, and as long as there is a market, there will be seller to take advantage of it.  
This, of course, lead to remember the Pet Rock craze, which lasted about six months and 
made the guy that came up with the idea of selling rocks with two eyes glued onto them a 
millionaire!
