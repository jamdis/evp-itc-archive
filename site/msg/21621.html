I've just finished two days at the American Ghost Society conference where I was blown away by a presentation by April Slaughter on Andy Coppock's ITC Probe.

What do you guys know about this?

She played a good portion of an eleven minute interactive, full sentence conversation that she had with her grandfather who died seven years ago.

Unless this is a hoax, which I would hope that an MIT graduate and biomedical engineer wouldn't be part of, this is extraordinary.

We heard full sentences in the same voice throughout. The words were understandable with intonation coming through.

I was utterly blown away.  

Coppock is doing a controlled study with it, using other scientists and a few ghost hunters.
