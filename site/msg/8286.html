Mike,

I haven't done that personally, because i don't think that the 
communication is at the point to receive full sentences.  You get a 
couple words here and there that we can piece together to make 
somewhat of a sentence, but i don't believe anybody has reached that 
stage yet.  If i got a full sentence that would be great, but it 
probably wouldn't be clear enough to post on the board.  If i have to 
explain what it says to convince people to hear it, than it' s 
probably nothing.  When in doubt, throw the file out.  I try to form 
the questions for the most part in Yes and No format, it seems they 
can always respond with yes and no.  I do get other stuff that is 
good, but i try and keep it simple for them.  And frank just sits 
back and listens without asking questions allowing them to get that 
message across.  I prefer to ask questions, because i know when to 
wait for a response.  The Skype session i had with my Joe's box last 
friday with Juli and Joel was a great example of getting good 
communication.  We were getting answers to all our questions and 
that "Frank Sumption" file was one of the clearest i have ever heard 
come through my Joe's Box #2, which is now broken again.  Juli has 
all the audio and will post more as she goes through it for everybody 
to hear.
