This would seem to illustrate the divergence between schools of
thought with traditional evp and itc/rtsc. While using a faraday cage
to to prevent spurious and unwanted RF signals from contaminating a
microphone would be helpful for traditional evp, would it not be
counterproductive for box type ITC/RTSC? I am not trying to be
sarcastic or anything, I am just wondering if the idea here is to
ensure that if anything did make it through that it would most likely
be genuine? I would be afraid that the RF shielding would be too
effective thus blocking communication with a linear sweep box. If you
did get communication from a box in a faraday cage, it would certainly
make the skeptics and cynics be forced to present new arguments.
