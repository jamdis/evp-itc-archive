Jerome,

I love this:

"I feel it's at least a *3 PART* dynamic and one which exists between the
device itself, the operator and those on the other side."

Very succinct and from my experience I find it to be quite true.  For me,
the most uncontrollable aspect of this dynamic is the device itself.  I use
Minibox Pluses and a Joe's Box because they work for me.  But, if you put a
Shack Hack in my hands, it works but not nearly as well.  Others experience
amazing results with Shack hacks.  I was experimenting with a Shack Hack
about a month back and the communication wasn't clear enough but I got the
gist of what they were saying.  I asked them to hold on while I switched
boxes and the conversation continued with the same guy from the other side.
 The conversations and communicators are there, always.  This is why I put a
premium on the equipment being used because I know that I already have a
connection with the other side that can be facilitated on any box designed
to do so, but only a few boxes have worked to my personal satisfaction so
far.
