The best way to deal with the naysayers is to ignore them. We will
always have people saying such things and fighting with them only
negatively effects your own contact field. In the end you will not
convince them of anything.

people. Some of those turn out to be the naysayers who want to argue,
I drop the conversation and move on.

don't know and feel enough to understand anything I would have to tell
them. They think it is a completely "technical" process, when the most
important components are the links between the two communicators.

As Misty and I have talked about in the past I was one who use to jump
at any opportunity to "fight the skeptics". I have since learned that
it is a pointless waste of energy and it does do damage to your
contact field or bridge if you have established one. The turmoil and
negative emotions controversy spawns are not conducive to the types of
communications we have been experimenting with.

Some of those skeptics genuinely want to learn what we know but just
can't grasp the basics needed to perpetuate this reality. Others just
want to get you worked up and off balance. By ignoring negative
contacts you are protecting your connection to your higher self. Don't
fall for their traps!
