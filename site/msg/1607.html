This time I made a small plastic echo box. I tried plastic years ago, and it 
didn't work, this time it seems to. The injection speaker accounts for the 
weaker voices I think, since it's an ear bud, and is 32 ohm, normal speakers 
are 8 ohms. More voices are in my folder.

This is fairly important, it means the echo box could be molded into a 
plastic cabinet, or the bottom of a plastic cabinet box could be used for 
the echo box, and the top for the board and batteries.
