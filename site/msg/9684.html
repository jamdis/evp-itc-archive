would require visual awareness.

"Can you name something nearby me?" or "Please tell me what I'm holding in my left
hand." A sophisticated multi-transmission two way radio would not explain answers

wouldn't be from a hoax. Again, I look to relevance to be key, and relevant info that
would not come easily to a stranger is even better.

For example, I turned on my MiniBox for the very first time and said, "Hello?" and
