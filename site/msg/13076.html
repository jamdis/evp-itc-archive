It depends on what is going on.  Since the focus on the investigation is 
more than just gathering EVPs  sometimes there needs to be more 
chatter.   Actual investigations can take hours and sometimes several 
visits, so what you see on a show is maybe 8-10 hours compressed into 45 
minutes.     As far as thanking the spirits, again it depends on what is 
needed and the personality of the investigator.

Researching at home is a lot different- at home you are in your comfort 
zone,  I have a residual energy that walks down my hallway, I have seen 
it a hundred times, and it never freaks me out.  But if I see something 
out on an investigation- in a strange location where I am out of my 
comfort zone, it will freak me out a little-- get the heart racing.  And 
I have been known to ask what the H-E double hockey sticks was that?  
Now I will run towards something- others instinctively  start to run 
away- again it depends the personality and experience of the 
investigator, lol and the vocabulary.

In a team envvironment usually and in my opinion, hopefully, you have a 
variety of styles and personalities.  I personally think that works 
better since you never know who might attract a response.
