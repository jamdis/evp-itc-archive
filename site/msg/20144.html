The digital sampling rate of the DR60 is only 8KHz, so that is one of the reasons it is muffled and noisy (excluding what the codec contributes in terms of artifacts). The QR200 has much better (higher) sampling rate and therefore does sound crisper, BUT if you put the QR200 on long play mode, it would equate to the DR60 in sound quality and maybe evp reception as well. Worth trying.
JEFF
