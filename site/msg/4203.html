I just created a folder (LC's RS 12-469 Recordings) to dump my 12-469 
experimental recordings into.  There is one recording in the folder 
that to me says, "Just work on it!"  Background: I was constructing 
the echo chamber and experimenting with the amped speakers running 
out of the earphone jack with an amped mic from my audio recorder, 
all together in a closed plastic project box. That's when I was 
recording the interior sounds and heard "Just work on it!" in a male 
voice upon playback of the audio.  I was frustrated at the time, so 
the message is spot on...what do you guys think?  

BTW, I left the 12-469 and audio recorder outside the box and have 
since tore apart the "My Lil Recorder" so I could run its speaker 
inside the box, but still control it from the outside. What is 
interesting is when I close the box and play just the white noise, it 
sounds like people talking in the box, but when I record it, it 
sounds like a siren! If I take the lid off, it does not sound like 
people talking, nor does it sound like a siren. Not sure what's going 
on, yet, but it does indicate that sound waves bouncing around in the 
chamber definitely sound much different than straight from the Radio 
Shack radio.
