As I said, we really don't know a lot about this field (no pun intended - 
or was it?)  Who has actually tried receiving some sort of radio/audio in 
a Faraday cage?  If it has been tried to any great extent without any 
success, I wouldn't bother. But if no one has tried it yet, theories 
pro or con don't mean much.

If someone tries it and doesn't get anything, al that means is that 
"whoever" didn't want to bother with that particular experiment.  And 
if some tries it and does get results, it means "whoever" did want to play 
along. I wouldn't say lack of results proves or disproves the existence of 
spirits any more than absence of apparitions in my living room does.

You'll still get white noise from the electronics itself in a Faraday 
cage.

The photographer and educator Fresd Picker used to have a big rubber 
stamp.  Someone would send him a letter asking if this or that would work. 
He would just use his big rubber stamp "TRY IT!" and then sign "Sincerely, 
Fred Picker" and mail it back to you. So that's my advice too, if you're 
so inclined.

Zack
