I'm thinking there's something wrong with the tuner. You should be able to 
see the buzzing on a scope, and trace it back. One problem I did find is 
that the audio limiter tends to increase the noise level. I think it's gain 
jumps up when no voice fragments are present, and over amplifies the noise 
between stations, the result is a contant noise level that buries the 
voices, but it's not like that buzz. I bypassed the limiter in box #12, and 
it works so good, I left the circuit off the newest system.
