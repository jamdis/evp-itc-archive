The unit now called Hertz was formerly called cycles per second, and is the
frequency of an electromagnetic signal. For instance, WLS radio in Chicago
operates on a frequency of 890 kilohertz.

A Gauss is a unit of magnetic field strength. It is not a measure of
electric or electromagnetic field strength, only the magnetic component.
Zack
