> I may draw some flack from this post but I feel this is a very 
important subject. What exactly are you doing when you use a ritual 
or prayer for protection? You are placing limitations on the session. 
What is the point of doing research in ITC if you are not willing to 
deal with all aspects of it? The enemy is not some nasty entity out 
there that wants to cuss and threaten you, it is your fear. All of us 
who have been doing this for any length of time come across, as 
Sumption calls them "paper tigers". They bark, bark, bark but have no 
real bite! Unchecked fear causes the most damage both psychologically 
and physically. This fear (the lack of control of it) is also the 
reason why people make up these rituals.Regards,Frank R.
