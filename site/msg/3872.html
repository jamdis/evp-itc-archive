I often use my Alinco DX-77 to tune in German or French, and 
sometimes Chinese, and use that as a raw audio using the foreign 
language method. You tune in a language you don't understand, 
announce your'e doing an EVP session and record from a short distance 
form the speaker. Often on play back there will be meaningful 
mesasages in your language mixed in with the orignial broadcast. It's 
contraversial, and people that speak the language used can't hear the 
messages.  i also like to tune between station on an AM radio when 
skip is coming in, that results in some clear and meaningful voices.
