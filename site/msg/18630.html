"So if someone is saying that something you have worked on for years is all
in your head while they completely ignore all evidence and results, you just
sit quiet, don't say a word cuz someone might get upset? "

No, it's that you don't say anything argumentative. You take the high road.
If you feel you must say something, you can say "we'll have to agree to
disagree," or "that is your opinion," etc, because it is just their opinion.
No need to get personal, insulting, emotional or overly defensive. If you
believe in what you are doing, what does it matter what anyone else in the
whole universe believes?

There is intelligent debate, and then there is petty arguments. Getting
petty does nothing for you or your cause, and only brings you down to your
opponents petty levels.

The old adage really holds true-if you can't say something nice, don't say
anything at all.
