Ann,

Thank you for your reply. I honestly appreciate your views and the
obvious thought put into your reply. 

You are right, there is no harmony meter, no dark-guy scale, no way to
effectively measure anything. What we do know, is that the "dark-guys"
do come through. Something happens that enable them as well as the
"light-guys" to speak to us in real time. As of yet, we are unable to
claim with any certainty the possible effects of either on our world.

I also agree that it is possible that they communicate through classic
EVP as well. However, the communication is not in real time and to the
best of my knowledge a give and take conversation rarely occurs. With
the communication capable of coming through the box, a relationship
begins to form. A trust that when one turns on the box, he/she will be
spoken to and be able to speak back in return. It is common belief
that the "dark-guys" can be quite deceitful. Why is that? Why not just
announce that they are the bad guys? There is a motive and intention
to disguise who they are. What are they capable of? We simply can not
say with any certainty.

I also can not say with any certainty that a portal can be opened. No
one can say that one can not be opened. I for one am not comfortable
with the "wait and see" attitude. I definitely see the positive
possibilities from any sort of EVP. However, I see the enormous
potential for negative. Enormous. To not see that is, IMO, short-sighted.
