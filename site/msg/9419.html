Hi Kay and Rees.  Just a quick suggestion about purchasing crystals ... if you get them 
from a local 'rock shop' you can save a bundle.  And I've recently purchased some beauties 
off eBay.  Some of them came on the slow boat from China (literally!) but they were worth 
the wait (about 2 months).  And each time, I received exactly the crystal that was shown.  
Just be sure to check the seller feedback for the ones who are delivering as promised.

Once crystals make their way into the commercial market and carry such labels as 
Lemurian and the like, they become (in many cases) priced astronomically high.  If you 
know what you're looking for, you might be able to find it for under $5.  I picked up a 
bunch of nice crystals in Las Vegas for super low pricing.  Something like $1 each or 
maybe it was 5 for $1 (it's been years, so I don't remember exactly, but it was very low).  
All it required on my part was digging through the bin to find the ones I was drawn to.

Margaret
