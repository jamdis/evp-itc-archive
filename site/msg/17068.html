Whenever I think I have an EVP, I always run a poll on all my message boards
to see first if people hear anything at all, then second, what it is they
think they hear.

If most people hear the same thing, then you can be reasonably certain it
isn't just pareidolia. On my last round, everyone unanimously agreed they
heard a male voice, but what they thought it said varied widely. But I was
satisfied because no men were present when it was recorded, so I know at
least there was some other voice there. In this case, what it said wasn't as
important as just knowing it was there.

If no one or only a few hear the same thing though, then I have to assume it
is just paredolia.
