The Trifield 100XE is a good all-around EMF meter. A lot of people use
them on ghost hunts. I've borrowed one from a friend once in a while.
You should do fine with it.

The Natural EMF meter is supposed to be more sensitive to fields in a
lower frequency range that are emitted by living things (or ghosts).
It is supposed to reduce sensitivity to the frequency used by house
wiring, I believe, so this does not affect it so much.

I think both are sensitive to the human range of fields.

Experiment with it and have fun!
