Does that bother you? Doing this work we all know it shouldn't. I 
have to ask before I say "join the club" LOL

Many involved in this research have experienced it at one time or 
another. For those who think they can turn on or turn off the spirit 
world or open/close some invisible doorway, it can be very unsettling 
to be communicated with when they arent expecting it and sometimes in 
ways they dont appreciate. 

If I am tryin to sleep thats not the time to try talking to me, 
alive, dead, etc.. makes no difference. But now and then its loud and 
clear enough to make me "look" for the speaker. Whispers in the 
ear "i love you" "can you hear me", or even "HEY WAKE UP" yelled in 
the ear... I am sure its funny to watch me run around and hunt for 
the invisible man/woman, I know when I talked with a few people (who 
are now passed) we always said that would be fun to do if we could 
come back to "haunt" people we would definitely give it our best 
shot. When I am wide awake and very much aware, it can be a different 
experience. Especially if they answer you back. 

I did once get a man who sang an old fashioned song I had never heard 
and played on his fiddle the most amazing melody. I was sick in bed 
listening to the rushing water from the fish tank when it suddenly 
changed and his singing got louder and louder...so the whole time I 
was hearing it I was saying to myself wow this is some fever. Hmmmm 
how high is this fever maybe I should get up and check again. Dang! 
That guy can really sing! I just checked my temperature 5 minutes ago 
(just under 101) it cant have changed that much surely.. This dude 
frickin rawks! Wow now he has a violin no wait thats a fiddle. Ok 
this is definitely a cool hallucination or I am dying. Either way 
bring it on I aint budging! So I finally just relaxed and stopped 
worrying if I was tripping and enjoyed the rest of his song. I 
decided later if I had been hallucinating perhaps I should try 
getting fevers more often. It was a little painful but the discomfort 
is just a distant memory...the song...well the song left quite a mark!

It's something I questioned myself about at first and them about over 
the years using various methods.
*h20 method = "your not causing those you're hearing from the 
fountain" 
*evpmaker method = "they're coming by the fountain"

They call the fish tank the fountain tho it has other connotations. 
*sweep tuning = "its good to drink the fountains of the knowledge"

I am kind of thirsty now that I mention it gonna go get a drink after 
I post a couple =)

Misty
