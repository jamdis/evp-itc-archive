I love how their website says, "This is a tool for use by experienced paranormal investigators only."  I think it should be the goal for almost EVERYONE to have a ghost box. Sure, some negative stuff can happen, but think of the possibilities of what we could learn if everyone had one of these things.  I'd think the positive would far outweigh the negative.  Either way, to say that the box should be in the hands of only a few, seems extremely arrogant to me.
