Well, the new TruTV series, Door to the Dead aired last night. I
recorded it with my DVR so I could watch and review the program video
a bit better than just watching it live.
So... what did I think of Door to the Dead?
Let me say this... the first episode was more entertaining than Ghost
Hunters, G.H.I., Paranormal State, and even that other one where the
guy and his team get locked over night in places (I can't think of
the name right now). Note: I don't watch any of them anymore, even
though I did give them all a try at least once or twice.

Anyway, although I had some problem with the entity 'orbs' portion of
the Door to the Dead investigation, since I may have seen an 'orb
source' when I put the DVR in super slow motion, the rest of the
series was put together nicely, with a good story line, good
interaction of characters, and some very entertaining and interesting
methods used for the variety of investigative tactics.

Unlike G.H., G.H.I., Paranormal State, and that other one, I'll
probably watch 'Door to the Dead' again to see how it pans out. The
new TruTV series caught my attention.
