It just takes a little time if you understand the basics.  All the 
different devices have their own nuances and peculiarities, advantages and 
disadvantages. AVR's run faster than PIC's due to architecture, and the 
code in an AVR for a given routine is generally 3/4 to 2/3 the size of a 
PIC program to do the same thing.

The AVR users' slogan, BTW, is "Come over to the dark side."
