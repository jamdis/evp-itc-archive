>                                                Just wondering if anybody has ever thought about making a wind tuned ghost box. 
>      My idea is to put a Theremin like charged antenna in a cardboard cylinder about 1 to 2 foot tall with a diameter of about 5 to 10 inches with a wind inlet at the bottom and a screen at the top. The wind would blow a light piece of material around in the cylinder that would change the capacitance seen by the antenna. I was thinking you could probably hook the thing to an old non-electronically tuned radio directly across the tuning capacitor or in place of it. At least thats what I would try first. It may need some sort of amplification and or filtering, don't know, just a hack electrician here. 
>      It would be nice if the light piece of material was a ball of hair from a loved one but i would guess that a piece or more of aluminized mylar would probably work better.
