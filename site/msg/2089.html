Years ago, when I was taping on a reel to reel, mostly, I often got  
voices that sounded maddeningly not like me, but like people I knew  
who were not dead. They never identified themselves as those people,  
but the voices were dead ringers (ar ar ar).  I am also, apparently,  
able to randomly project my voice into the minds of people I know. It  
begins to happen to almost everyone that I bond strongly with. I  
can't tell you how many times I have heard someone I know spin around  
and say, "What?" They always say they hear my voice, but don't  
understand what I am saying. This is always accompanied, on my end,  
by a sort of ringing sound in my head. I cannot control it at all.  
I'm just throwing this out there as food for thought that may pertain  
to your discussion.....
