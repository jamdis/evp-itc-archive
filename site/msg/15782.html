I think it varies depending on who you ask as well as what they have personally experienced. As an example, some people believe in angels and demons others do not. Therefor some may get messages that correspond to their own beliefs as well as the beliefs of those who are speaking.

Almost every person who does this eventually develops a theory that may change over time based on what they experience and what their beliefs are. Then there are those who remain undecided or have doubts.

As for me I look at it as purely educational, a learning experience. Ive met many people I never knew existed. Ive researched topics I'd never even considered. Ive gotten messages and images that are awe inspiring and many interesting concepts to ponder and explore.

There are many glimpses of truth, as well as momentary proof along the way that lead me to develop my own theory and eventually you will develop your own too. This research is fascinating and can be quite mind blowing.

I dont worry what anyone else thinks. I have no need to prove anything to anyone else. They can do that for themselves. I always say honesty is the best policy and if you are honest with yourself and others the only ones who object are those who's lies arent tolerated. So just look for the truth yourself and dont let me or anyone else tell you what that is. 

It's a great journey and your path is your own!
