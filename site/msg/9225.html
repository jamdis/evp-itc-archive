Hi Jeremy.  Congrats on such great EVP's!!  It hasn't happened often, but I have heard 
laughing on both my own recordings, and that of others, but nothing there was nothing 
sinister sounding.  And one time, I heard what sound like a kiss.

If the giggle sound was that of a young girl, maybe she was just tickled to be talking on 
the recorder?  If you think about it, it's very common for children to laugh and be silly 
while in the physical.  Makes sense they would do that in spirit as well.    :-)

Again, congrats on getting clear messages so quickly.  At some point, you'll want to make 
sure others can hear some of them too.  It's not uncommon for something to be heard 
absolutely clearly by the person doing the recording but not heard clearly (or even heard 
at all) by others.  I never really know how clear what I have might be until I get feedback 
from others.  It's my opinion that doing EVP work can definitely increase one's 
clairaudience.
