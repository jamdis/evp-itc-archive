Reports: Iranian Passenger Jet Crashes

Share Comments
An Iranian passenger jet with at least 105 people on board has crashed in bad weather, state media have reported.

The plane came down near the northwestern city of Urumiyeh, Iranian media said.
The semi-official Fars news agency said there were 105 people on board, but state TV put the number at 156.
Fars reported that 50 people had survived the crash.
