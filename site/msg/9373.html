I dunno gang. Everyone who knows me for any length of time knows that  
I have some real issues with the veracity of *some* of what went on  
during this time with this particular group. I was involved in the  
EVP community at the time and heard about it unfolding. I'm a  
journalist who specializes in things like behavioral science, so I am  
pretty capable of separating my own feelings and beliefs and hopes  
from facts. Before I get tarred again as a skeptic, I should also add  
that, at the time, I very much wanted everything emitting from this  
group to be true. All I can say is, I remain unconvinced that there  
wasn't hoaxing going on *to some extent*. There was also genuine  
stuff happening. I'm not here to say what was what--I'm just saying,  
approach this with an analytical mind to make your own judgement.

I'm never popular for stating this, but I remain convinced of my own  
conclusions, although I am always open to any new evidence.
