A model for TransCommunication (TC)
by Rolf-Dietmar Ehrhardt

After long-standing study of the phenomena in the EVP field and in 
ITC in general, I came to the conclusion that there must be 
principles behind the diverse results received by different 
experimenters. Much and perhaps everything that takes place probably 
has an explanation. Also in this field the basic principle is valid: 
you can not create something out of nothing. Nevertheless the 
question is: Are there common characteristics for these different 
results in TransCommunication? I believe it is the resonance between 
similar or identical thought structures or states of consciousness 
which makes a communication possible. Please bear with me during my 
attempt to set up a verifiable model.

Starting position of the experimenters:

After observing the experimenters in the fields of EVP and ITC, I 
believe it is reasonable to carry out a certain division into 
different personality types of experimenters. These types 
distinguish mainly through their experimentation behavior, which is 
also an expression of their attitudes concerning this phenomenon, 
and they distinguish also in the achieved results and the procedures 
used while experimenting. A typical experimenter might have 
qualities of two or three of these personality types in their own 
personality make-up.

Mourning Experimenter

This type represents an experimenter who does not permit himself 
other thoughts than those related to the deceased loved one caused 
by the strong emotional attachment to that person. This experimenter 
is strongly focused on his desire to get in contact with that loved 
one in spirit.
It makes a big difference whether it is love or fear. For example, a 
surviving wife might fear economic or emotional hardship as a result 
of her husband's death, or she might fear her own death. If fear 
represents the main motive, unpleasant situations can occur. ( 
See "How can I avoid unpleasant situations"). Experimenters that 
undertake the contact experiment from love and attachment can expect 
pleasant results, although it may take time to achieve a contact.
This experimenter is mostly very tolerant opposite the results of 
others and knows that the result depend much on himself. Although he 
does not know how the contacts are accomplished he chooses the right 
way: he is focused because of his situation.
In the beginning these contacts are mostly of relatively good 
quality, but decrease more and more in quality, duration and 
frequency, unless this type of experimenter becomes a "systematic 
experimenter".
  

Spontaneous or Impulsive Experimenter

This person wants to get many good voices, even fragments of 
sentences or words, regardless of the content. He is not interested 
in background information and doesn't care how, why, why not 
different, which connection is there?
If an experimenter of this kind learns that another experimenter 
achieved good voices with a specific device or a device combination, 
he soon duplicates these devices, buying the necessary equipment to 
receive these same good voices. In case he has success with these 
devices only once, he modifies immediately again in order to better 
the results again. Every preserved signal and voice fragment is 
analyzed and interpreted, to find any connection with something. He 
speaks in this case also about "science", "scientific 
approach", "critical point of view" and so on.
That is the way people act who call other experimenters liars if 
they achieve good and even better results while approaching the 
whole matter from another point of view (for example, the spiritual 
way rather than the technical way).
  

Systematic Experimenter

This experimenter has studied the relations between spiritual 
conditions and results of the experiments. He has transferred his 
knowledge, earned through experience, into daily experimental work. 
If these experimenters should still have a surpassingly high 
mediality, highly amazing results are to be achieved then.
If this experimenter succeeds, holding his ego in check, he can set 
up remarkable results and (if the reader wants it) also findings for 
mankind.
This experimenter is exposed to extreme attacks since the achieved 
results are put down as fraud, delusion or mischief mostly by other 
ones.
Mostly it is envy which leads to these attacks. Because as soon as 
the voice critics receive the same or similar quality (even it is 
only a fragment), these are represented immediately as the ultimate 
results of a new experimentation technique and praised in the 
highest. The critics act this way although they drop back a short 
time later again onto the old result state. Because otherwise they 
would have come much further in quality, duration and frequency of 
voices. 

These different types of experimenters have existed from the very 
beginning of the EVP/ITC scene. A tolerance of the other 
experimenters and their results is practiced rarely. As a matter of 
fact there are also mixed types. Those individuals are possibly 
changing and evolving from one type to another.

The mourning experimenter is less interested in the quarrels and 
more interested in reassurance that there is life/existence after 
physical death.

The systematic experimenter with his mostly overriding results 
withdraws after sad experiences and gives insight into the results 
of his contacts to at best a small group of people.

The experimenter acting spontaneously is interested mostly in 
finding out who has voices recorded so that he can test also 
something new as quickly as possible.
