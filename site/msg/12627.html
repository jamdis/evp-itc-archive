> While your on youtube you may want to check out some other peoples 
> videos on how to modify an existing radio. Franks modified a number 
> of different types as have others like Steve Hultay. Steve posted a 
> list of the ones he's been able to modify. Usually it requires 
> nothing more than snipping  the mute wire. The archives on this group 
> are a wealth of information just use the keyword search.
> 
> You also may want to try out manual sweep Most people find that 
> brings in very good results. For manual sweep you are just sweeping 
> the band. I prefer to use a radio with a knob dial. Then just start 
> turning it back and forth across the band quickly so it doesnt have 
> quite enough time to tune in any one radio station.
> 
> I also have had quite a bit of success using a dead zone spot in 
> between any stations so that its set to off station and all I hear 
> live is the radios static/whir. I have posted examples of some of 
> these methods off my website.
