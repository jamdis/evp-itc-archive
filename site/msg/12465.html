By-the-way:
I've got the new version of the 12-470 (black case) torn apart on my 
bench again.  I know I failed to get it hacked the first time (last 
year), but I figured I'd try it again.  It is totally different than 
the others... and they have made sure you can't tell what the radio 
chip is by getting rid of the markings and covering both sides of the 
circuit board with a waxy substance. I've been carefully scraping it 
away. The chip is a 30 pin device, possibly a KK22425 or KK22426 or 
equivilent.
The mute pin for the #25 is pin 2 but looking at the connection 
scheme itmight not be the right one.
I guess I'll mess with it until I break it... or ???

I'll put some pictures of the new 12-470 circuit board in my photo 
area of this froum.
