Hi Bruce~

I understand the light heartedness of the "family hour" posts. I don't
take any issue with that at all. The families of those of us involved
in the paranormal HAVE to be a bit different then the average Joe's or
Smith's. I only referred to that topic because of the other stuff
mentioned- negativity and dark ones.

There is absolutely nothing in place to safe guard who purchases the
box and who doesn't besides the cost. If you've got the money or a
credit card, the box is yours- whoever "you" are.

I don't think we should presume anything about the purchasers of the
box. I know people in the filed who have never experienced anything
evil or dark and wouldn't know what to do if they did. Admittedly,
they can be and will be deceitful. 

I am in full support of further research and groups and individuals
working together. However, IMHO, I think this falls into a different
category. Morally and ethically, I think it is short sighted to
approve of or promote in any way, the distribution of this device at
this time. 

When we imagine the possibilities, we need to imagine them all.

Babs
