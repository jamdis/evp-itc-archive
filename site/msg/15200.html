Sweep is making the radio tune across the band, the modes are random, linear or manual. Random jumps arounds around the band-randomly, the idea being that the entities would comtrol the sweep by affecting the white noise transistor, and they did say they do that. Linear is a smooth sweep up, then back down the band, the entities seem to have an easier time tracking the linear sweep. Manual is done by hand on a cheapy radio with a tuning knob, it's easier wiht a radio that has a tuning knob that does the half turn from one end of the band to the other. 

 When I first started using linear mode, I couldn't hear any of the usual voices, then all the sudden, I could hear it. So I'm uncertain if it's the brain that has to adjust, of what if you have to create/allow the possiblity? I used to use radnom exclusivley, and that worked well, now I get litte with random mode. 

 I use linear now becuase it's much easier to get the circuit to work. Random can be finicky, as you're trying to get random pulses to be amplified, and passed through the circuit.
