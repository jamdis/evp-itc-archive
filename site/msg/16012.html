I heard from my dad today finally!  I knew it was him, because when I asked how many people we were talking to today, the answer was, "Just just talk to me!"  My dad was always impatient, always interrupting people, so this was exactly what he would say.  Then he answered with his name to confirm.  When I asked if my dad's friend Gordy was there, the minute I mentioned his name, I swear I heard "asshole."  It's possible my dad wasn't getting along with Gordy, because Gordy had a tendency in life to grate on my dad's nerves, so this might be something he'd say.  

Then I asked for him to say my mom's name, and he responded by saying her name in a way that was exactly how he used to say it when he was teasing her.  It wasn't his voice, it was radio voice, but it was the same inflection he used.  When I asked what relationship he had to us, there was a long sentence I couldn't make out, something about me growing up, and then, "We know Jeffrey," which is my dead brother's name -- followed by "daughter." 

I asked if it was my dad, and a clear answer:  Yes, followed by two very loud, clear, "I love yous."  

But the best part was this:  I asked if he had a message for my mom (who was sitting there, too), and he said, "Love you.  Don't wait for.  Me.  You will be.  Much.  Older.  I love you.  Love.  You."  How awesome is that?!!!!  

If this wasn't my dad, it was certainly a kind, loving entity, that's for sure.  Just wanted to share with you all.  (Now I really need to figure out how to get these tapes into my computer!!!)
