One thing I'd suggest: remove any question(s) that you are asking, from the audio file.  Since you're trying to study how different people will interpret the same file, and are trying not to give any hints as to what other people hear in the file, leaving the question in could be a factor there.  For example, if you are asking a yes/no question in the audio, then the people listening might be inclined to restrict their answers to having heard a "yes" or a "no" (because the question implies that is what may follow).  But by leaving the question out, you remove any hints as to what they should expect.
