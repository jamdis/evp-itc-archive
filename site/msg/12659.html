Full credit to Steve Hultay for discovering the hackability of this
radio, and as he had mentioned in the past, it really wasn't the
easiest. After tearing it apart, cutting, desoldering, etc...to locate
the mute wire/pin/solder pad, I found out that it can be one of the
easiest hacks out there. 

Rather than disassembling and such, simply cut the trace on each side
of the mute pin solder pad. Takes about 30 seconds with a hobby knife.
I felt really stupid after 4 hours taking everything apart and
desoldering wires, etc...

instructions with photos can be found on my blog if interested. 

http://blog.myspace.com/index.cfm?fuseaction=blog.view&friendID=405441717&blogID=445524854

antenna options...
