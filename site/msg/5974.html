Babs,

I personally have no problem discussing what I am doing with respect 
to the RS boxes, and think you make some valid points. Many are open 
to ideas/theories, etc.

However, I suspect your posts come off a bit "bullying."  I 
understand it being difficult to express one's feelings/intent 
through a keyboard, but perhaps your style or maybe zeal for "the 
truth" has you sounding a bit "attacking" and very "defensive."  ;) 

I suggest that many here are at different places with respect to 
capabilities, ideas, motivations, knowledge, etc.  Some will welcome 
your ideas, while others will not see the need for them.  Everyone 
has their own place and purpose, and that's OK.  

Perhaps, a group put together by you who wish to suscribe to certain 
protocol designed to answer your questions might be best.  I do not 
think that you (or anyone for that matter) will be able to hammer out 
specific protocol for this entire group of experimenters to adhere to.

LC
