I would add that, in my limited experience, I've found that if you are the sensitive type, and have some psychic ability, using the box can somehow increase that talent.  At least that's what happened to me.  It can be disturbing to have contact with (what I presume are) dead people you don't know, and have them infringe upon your life when you are not expecting it.  So keep that in mind and be prepared that it might happen.  Otherwise, I agree with everything April said.  We had a lot of activity in our house after my dad died, and as soon as we acknowledged him, the activity backed off a bit.  

I would also add that my friends and family think I'm crazy, too, when I talk about this stuff.  But as soon as I conned my best friend and mother into doing a session, they believed.  Until someone experiences a ghost box session, it's hard to fathom that the person talking about it isn't schizophrenic.
