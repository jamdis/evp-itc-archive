Ursula,

I agree completely. For a hack, I have yet to find one that works better and
I have tried several.  It's cheap and sounds good and the popcorn isn't too
distracting.  This is the fallback hack.

On an investigation, I usually try to use one of the linear boxes I have (a
Minibox Plus, a Joe's Box, and a Franks) for the first session.  If we do
subsequent sessions, it is usually on a hack because everyone in the group
is used to the hack sounds whereas the linear boxes require an ear that uses
them more frequently for real time conversation.  Several people in my group
use GB's (5 total), but I am the only won with linear boxes.  So even though
we access to (arguably) better boxes for GBC, in a group situation the other
investigators (with a few exceptions) prefer the step sweep sound of the 125
because its what they know.
