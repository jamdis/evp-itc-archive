The antenna on Rosemary's Mini-Box is simply a telescoping antenna
like off an AM/FM radio attached to a banana plug.  The antenna unit
loosely plugs into the top of the wire antenna connector on the
Mini-Box.  That antenna was on prototype #3, the first demonstration
model, but dropped in favor of a wire antenna on the production models.
