Hi Margret,

yes, acoustical soundwaves - this could of course be a possible focus
point for manipulation by 'them'. However, I'm not sure if this really
takes place. Some time ago I had made some experiments to find out if
the sound is changed for EVPs. I compared the original signal to the
signal which had been transferred acoustically, and except for the
reverberations from the room etc. I could never hear any significant
differences; thus a manipulation of soundwaves did not take place and
seemed to be unnecessary for the production of EVP. This led me to the
conclusion that 'they' usually don't manipulate our physical
surrounding.
