Hi again Mike E.

I record alot of water sessions so by speaking during these sessions I often drown out or destroy whats being said. I am also careful not to record on full mic volume (I use half volume on the mic at all times) because the water noise will drown out the underlying voices even tho its 15+ feet away from the mic.

Franks right theres little need to speak but it does make it seem more "real" or proof positive to some people to get the direct reply to thier own voice. If you feel it interferes with your sessions when you speak there are ways around it. Try asking the question in your head or make a list using wordpad of your questions to save with the files if you dont want to forget what ya asked. Just do what you feel comfortable with.

Misty
