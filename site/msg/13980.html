I have to second what Frank mentioned.  The EVP Maker/allophones combo is excellent, in my opinion.  I know a lot of folks do not like it, but I find it utterly fascinating.  It is an animal all it's own so I can't really compare it to radio sweep, however, I do recommend people trying it to see if it might be a good match for them.  I've been working with it since Stefan created the allophones file and have been consistently amazed at the results.  

Of all methods I've tried, I have the most success with "real-time, two-way" communication via this combination.  It took some getting used to, hearing the relevant parts while ignoring the rest, but once I got the hang of that it become more and more easy as I worked with it.

Frank, it's great to read there's a possibility of some inexpensive FB's coming along.  Very cool.
