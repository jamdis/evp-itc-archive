I like what everyone is saying regarding this subject, because people 
are at different places with respect to one's own spirituality. I 
agree with Frank, because mankind has a tendency to create, or 
rather "mis-create" through fear.  Yet, I also believe some people 
need to believe in protection from spirits of the light, so fear does 
not take hold.  I look at it like this: All of us believe we are 
protected who are running these experiments; either by our own power 
and ability, or by some other unseen entity...or both. If we give 
anything power, we become its slave.  

I truly have no problem with either idea, as it just could be that 
those from the light assist, protect and guide us, because many of us 
on earth do not know just how much power we really have. Fear 
typically causes us to surrender our power over and "mis-think."  

Thus, I say:  Whatever works to keep a person free of fear is good 
with me.
