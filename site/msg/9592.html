Hi Holly.  I have Audacity, but prefer to use SoundSoap2 for noise reduction.  Audacity seems 
to leave an annoying high pitched whine-squeak-warble artifact no matter how low the noise 
reduction feature is set.  I know others with PC's (I use mostly a MAC) also like Clear Voice for 
noise reduction.  With SoundSoap2 or Audacity, you select a small sample area of the clip and 
have the program "learn noise" then apply it to the entire clip.  

Here's a link to the Bias/SS2 software video which gives a rundown of this very simple to use 
application:

http://www.bias-inc.com/downloads/videos/soundSoap2/
