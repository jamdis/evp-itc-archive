This device is misused by almost everybody!  This is because the manual
sucks and the TV people use it wrong consistently.  To figure it out, I
contacted the manufacturer a couple years back and this is what they told
me.

Always us the Trifield in Magnetic mode.  If the box is stable and there is
no lightning, the box should not sound an alarm unless something paranormal
is happening (or you move your digital recorder to close to it, magnetized
speakers).  If you use electric or combination, you can, and will, pick up
the body electric with every motion and may receive many false positives.
 The meter should also never be used while walking because this too gives
unreliable readings.  There is nothing worse than seeing some TV schmuck
holding it in their hands and claiming something paranormal is going on
because an alarm is sounding, never mind that the camera is right next to it
emitting magnetism through the mic / internal speaker.

Also, the name trifield is a misnomer.  It only has one plane of reading,
not three!  The yellow one (AC) has three fields.  The natural trifield only
measures the plane that extends from the top and bottom edges lengthwise
from the devices.  So if you have the device sitting upright, you will only
measure whatever is above the device.  If you have it laying flat, you will
be able to measure the full plane of length extending beyond the bottom and
top edges.  Therefore, it is best to use this device slightly elevated and
laying flat.

Using it in this fashion has yielded some amazing results thus far.  While
it is rare for this meter to fire when used properly, when a "spirit"
understands how to trigger the alarm you can get conversations that can last
upwards of an hour.  I have experiences this only 6 times in all the years I
have investigated.  Most of the time, this device yields a single alarm, or
maybe two, with no real time interaction with questions.
