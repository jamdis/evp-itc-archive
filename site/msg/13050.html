I am looking for 5 - 10 researchers to voluntarily participate in an effort that will become a part of the mini-documentary I am planning to begin filming later this month.  When completed early in 2009 I will be placing this video on my Youtube site for all to view. Freely sharing our research is the key!  If you are one that participates, I'd like to be able to mention you and display your work in the video.
The subject of the research is:
Attempted spirit contact using an object associated with a disaster as a catalysis for a specific contact... away from the scene of the disaster.
If selected, the volunteer researcher will be mailed a small amount of material associated with the aircraft crash in question.  This crash debris was obtained after all litigation was complete and a salvage company had purchased all debris from the airline company.
Note: There are no health hazards associated with the debris.
Note: The detailed information about the crash is not being giving at this time in order to make it a controlled ITC experiment.  This way the audio files I receive from participants may or may not contain information about those who perished or information about their life.  I alone know that information at this time.
It won't take much of your time.  All I ask from the selected researchers are five, 30 second audio files of attempted spirit contact, using a method of the researcher's choice.  The crash debris you will be provided must be used in association with the attempted contacts. (Close proximity to the recording device is encouraged.)
You can e-mail me the audio files, along with a text explanation of how the files were captured when you complete you research. (Method used, how debris was used, etc.)
If you are interested in volunteering, please send me a private e-mail through this forum and tell me a bit about yourself and what method you currently use.  If you are one of those selected I will reply to your e-mail, asking for a mailing address to send the object debris (which will arrive in a small padded envelope.)  I don't need the debris back, so keep it if you wish...
Thanks!
- Mike C. (UFO Geek) www.ufogeek. com

Below is some information about the video.
A new documentary coming in early 2009
"Voices From The Scorched Earth"... A paranormal documentary ("Paradoc") about contact with those who perished in a commercial jetliner crash which claimed all on-board.
The 4g+ crash impact being so violent, all passengers and crew died instantly.  They had only a matter of seconds prior to their terrifying vertical plunge. Witnesses described the aftermath scene as more gruesome than they had ever experienced.
Some say spirit communication is more likely to occur if a person had suffered a quick, tragic death, with minimal time to prepare but enough time for emotional thought and feelings to run high, such as what happened in this tragic accident.
The incident took only seconds, but according to the official report there was enough time for very high emotion to have been felt by the passengers and crew.
With this theory in mind,  Mike will perform ITC sessions at the scene of the crash (exact impact point), and will be using objects from the crash as part of some ITC sessions.
During ITC sessions a number of paranormal devices will be used, some designed by Mike and currently in the prototype stage.
Mike will begin filming "Voices From The Scorched Earth" in late December 2008.  Watch for this Paradoc on his Youtube website (Youtube.com/ ufogeek), early in 2009.
