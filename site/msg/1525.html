I think that voice was from a later box. I made two of these boxes in a 
ttempt to make them smaller, and portable. The number a the begiing is 
usually the order received or heard. The other one is AM/FM, but the AM 
tuner takes a screwy tuning voltage, so it doens't work right. I want to 
just add a different AM tuner, and keep the FM tuner. That one has the steel 
box too, but I don't remember if it's the wave guide box.

All of the later Boxes are made so the Echo box can be easily replaced, and 
some have front panel jacks to allow the use of other echo boxes. I quite 
putting in those jacks, as most had no understanding of their use, even if I 
explained it.
