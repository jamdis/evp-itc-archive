Speaking of Lily Dale... My youngest dachshund is named Lily Dale and  
she was just a puppy when I was talking to the so called "Fish Group".  
They LOVED her. I would crate her at night in my bedroom and we could  
hear "them" talking to her. They'd also rustle the newspapers in the  
very large crate I had down. She'd look at the direction of the  
voices, but I could tell she couldn't see anything and was very  
confused. I always wondered if her name had something to do with it.  
Or that she was a puppy. They didn't speak to my other 2 as far as I  
know.

I'm glad they were nice to her and genuinely seemed to like talking to  
her. Weird, huh?
