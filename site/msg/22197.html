I think just about every researcher can expect others to continue the
research. I believe in giving credit where credit is due, but I'd be a fool
to believe that no one else had anything good to contribute. People are
going to try their own experiments with the boxes. They may or may not find
some surprising things. That remains to be seen. But I would never squelch
others' research.

I don't think everyone wants to use them for paranormal investigation
(ghost hunting) "in the dark" and not all paranormal investigators are
doing it to be scared. I don't scare easily and fear is the last thing on
my mind in an investigation. Also, I do not believe everyone is out to
steal your claim to fame as the early researcher of the box. But realize
there may be a few who have something useful to contribute and we may yet
learn something from their work.

Zack
