If you ever saw the series SciFi Investigates, which briefly ran
earlier this year, you may have seen the episode with Mark. This
series focuses on debunking claims of the paranormal, whether it was
the reported crash of a ufo in the desert (the 1951 Roswell
incident)or the existence of the afterlife. Well, the show with Mark
was the only one I remember where they couldn't logically explain how
the double image photos were forming.

And that is an issue we face when we present Box recordings.
Non-believers try to explain away the recordings by applying standard
scientific based arguments. They say we're hearing broadcast
fragments, or it's just matrixing, whatever. But even though we use
readily available technology to build the Box, the results that are
achieved go beyond conventional science. We don't yet understand how
it is working, but it is. As you use the Box, any version of it, you
will discover that certain messages intended for the operator come
through more frequently.

-Bill
