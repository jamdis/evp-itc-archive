While I've learned a lot from some others about tips for doing 
investigations, I don't think there is a "right way" or "wrong way" you 
can't find out for yourself as long as you keep basic safety and manners 
in mind - e.g. treat everyone (whether they be living people or spirits) 
with respect; be careful in the dark to know how you might get hurt and be 
careful; etc. You would learn by yourself with time what works for you and 
what doesn't.

and three below.  No matter how nice someone seems to be, it may not be 
so.  A rule I saw somewhere emphasized this - "A person who is nice to you 
but is not nice to the waiter is NOT a nice person."
