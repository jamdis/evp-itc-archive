It would be tough to add the amp to a hack radio, I added it to the tuner
after it was unmanufactured. You have to pull the tuner, add some turns of
wire around the antena rod, bring some leads out of the case and make a
little add on box outside the radio. Might be easier, but more expensive to
get an external antenna, like C-Crane's twin coil ferrite antenna, or RS's
amplfied AM antenna. You should be able to get AM skipo at night.
