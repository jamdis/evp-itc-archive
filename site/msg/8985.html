Hi Bruce.  That's wonderful your are in a financial position to be so generous. :-) 

I don't know if we can really go so far as to say that people who buy from eBay are 
receiving units with the wrong kind of energy attached, just because profiting from sales 
isn't a course of action you or I, nor some others would take.  If that is the case, where do 
we draw the line?  I paid a hefty price for my MiniBox, as have many others.  I'm sure there 
is some profit involved there.  The energy of it seems just fine to me and I hold no 
disregard toward Ron for recognizing there is a market for his devices.

Perhaps those who log onto eBay looking to find something so they can experiment with 
automated sweep feel it's their lucky day when they discover such a listing?  Maybe they 
even feel it's a 'sign' that it's the right thing for them to do some research.  And as far as 
the seller is concerned, we don't know what their attitude or motivation is, unless we know 
them personally.  Reminds me of a person on the cleaning crew who used to steal the food 
from the fridge at one of my corporate jobs.  Everyone was so harsh and judgmental about 
it, until one day, we learned she was feeding her hungry children!

Even if the person selling the units is the most greedy person in the world, I fully believe it 
is far more the attitude and intention of the person conducting the experiments which 
influence the energy of communication devices, than the attitude of the people selling the 
devices.  It's my opinion WE are the ones who attract what comes through to us on our 
equipment, much more so than the equipment itself.  

Margaret
