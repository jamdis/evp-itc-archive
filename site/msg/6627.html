I don't know what to tell you, Juli. The first repeat is the raw  
audio. The second is slightly slowed and the third is more slowed.  
Nothing else has been done. I posted a section of raw audio at your  
request.

I am using a minibox, not a hack. The minibox plugs directly into an  
Olympus DM-10 recorder--so direct audio from the box goes there  
without any ability by me to change it. The minbox also has a jack  
for an external microphone. I use a broadcast quality microphone--not  
a cheap one. That also feeds into the Olympus, which records in stereo.

The file is taken off the Olympus and opened in TwistedWave, a Mac- 
based sound editing suite. The only use are cutting, pasting,  
slowing, and occasionally amplifying and converting from stereo to  
mono. I can silence tracks, but I haven't been doing that since the  
first couple session back before xmas. There is no noise reduction  
feature in this suite, so obviously I am not using it.

I know that my files sounds much different from some others on this  
list, but I can't help that. This is what I am getting and I'm  
honestly trying to report that. I am a journalist, so I do try to  
live up my profession's ethics.

Ann
