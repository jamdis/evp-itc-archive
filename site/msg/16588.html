I've been making pc boards with Datak negative chemistry for 20 years. Datak
ER-71 is the photoresist and ER-8 is the developer.

I make a negative of the artwork using CorelDraw and print it with a laser
printer on transparency film. I usually have to touch up or darken a few
areas, then contact print it on the board coated with photoresist (exactly
according to the Datak instructions) under sunlight for about 2 to 3
minutes, then develop and etch.
