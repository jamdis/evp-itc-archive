Thanks--again! The tuner is just as it came out, nothing added. I usually add an exteranl antenna winding on the ferrite after I get he sweep working. Most of these tuners sound ok like this, but when swept they get more noisy. This model has a metal shield under the tuner, so I'm leaving that intact. 

 I'm not like an engineer or anything, and I have a hard time with maht, electronics is all math. Most of the circuits I use are straight out of the data sheets for the chips, or found on the web, like at Elliot sound http://sound.westhost.com/   Alot of it is intuition, just kinda know how to make it work, somehow. 

 I get most of the tuners/radios at thrift stores, and it's a crap shoot to get good ones.
