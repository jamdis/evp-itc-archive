I am an RF (primarily microwave) engineer.  I built a device some time ago 
called the Scalar Electrostatic Gradiometer:

http://amasci.com/freenrg/grado.html

This device definitely reacts to paranormal activity in many meanings of 
the word. It detects fields which are not normal electromagnetic fields. 
I invite all to build one and see for yourselves. Note there is an RF 
sniffer part of the circuit to indicate whether normal RF fields are 
present at the time. The indicated 1N914 is good up to the VHF range but 
I found that substituting a good microwave hot-carrier diode such as half 
of an HSMS8202 gives you a useful indication of the presence of RF fields 
into the microwave range if you use good microwave construction 
practices.
