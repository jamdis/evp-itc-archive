It's just a part of the circuit that Bob Shannon designed and built. If 
you are looking for scalar waves, you might want to verify that what 
you're detecting isn't normal RF. Scalar waves do not show up on a normal 
RF or EMF detector.  Also, it would indicate the presence of a radio 
transmitter in the area strong enough to possibly affect recording 
equipment. My Olympus VN960PC does pick up radio stations in its audio 
when I'm within a couple hundred feet of an antenna.
