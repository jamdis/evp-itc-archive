My long standing motto has been "Eff 'em if they can't take a joke".  There are so many people faking evidence and no one calls them out for it.  EVP is electronic voice PHENOMENA.  It's up to the listener to decide what is, where it comes from and what it means.  Ultimately, there's no way to know where the messages are coming from or what is being conveyed.  It's just there.  

I also agree that who says ghosts, spirits, aliens, whatever can't have a sense of humor?  I know that if I were out there "haunting" someplace and had any say about it, I'd be doing silly things.  I've often said to my husband that if he outlives me and weird stuff starts happening, it's me messing with him.  

I understand your anger, though.  Why is it that in certain fields and areas of interst that everyone is an "expert".  I'm the first one to admit I'm a newbie.  I'm skeptical, but find paranormal research fascinating.

As for the people who want your inventions, I say... "Make your own"!  It's one thing for someone to recreate something that's already been created, but quite another to want something for nothing (and then use it for personal gain as well).
