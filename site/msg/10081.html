Ann, 

I think it is safe to say that everyone has their own methodology of 
recording and how they handle the captured audio files.  Nothing 
wrong with that, as being allowed to experiment, be creative, and 
think out of the "box" is of great value, IMHO.

The issues with any of this is that again, people will demand to have 
their own paranormal/spiritual experiences as "proof."  Yet, we 
cannot prove it to anyone, as the "evidence" will never be good 
enough.  I would rather focus (as I am lead to work with said 
devices) on how I believe it is to be used by me.  Therefore I allow 
everyone else the grace to work with these however they deem.  

I think there is value in being able to share our ideas, triumphs, 
failures, etc.  That is how results will improve, by bouncing ideas, 
trying new things and working together.  If rule and law are put in 
place for everyone, then we limit creativity which is needed to 
overcome obstacles through invention.  

There will always be skeptics and critics, but one thing seems 
certain: everyone has their own time when they become open to the 
possibility of the paranormal/spiritual existing.  I just choose to 
share with those whose time has come.  :)

LC
