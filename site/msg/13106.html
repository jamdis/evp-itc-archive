AM is the kicker.  With amplitude modulation, and high power (being close to the tower) any device will pick up those voices.

AM basically is the same as real speech except that the carrier is not air but a relatively high frequency EMF.  FM does not have this problem because the words and music are created by varying the frequency and not the amplitude.  (a primer for those not into the geeky side of radio)

Don
