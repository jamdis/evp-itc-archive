>The design I came up with is much simpler. A flame is used as the 
>'vibrating element'. A phototransistor from Radio Shack is used in place of 
>a microphone. Of all the tests I ran, a simple oil burning lamp has worked 
>best. The lamp must have a flat, wide wick 3/4 to one inch across (very 
>important). The phototransistor will function with the phantom voltage 
>supplied from most digital recorders or sound cards. Very simple concept, 
>most people who see it are amazed at the simplicity. You will not find 
>plans on the internet except on AAEVP, I published an article almost two 
>years ago for thier news letter. Since that time I have simplified the 
>design to the two parts I have mentioned.
