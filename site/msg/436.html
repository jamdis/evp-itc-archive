Hi Frank and all,
I know what you mean, Frank.  It's very frustrating sometimes to play something most of us in the field would consider quite clear only to be met with blank stares.  When I met up with a couple of old friends on my vacation to PA (I hadn't seen them in 10 years) I introduced them to EVP.  They walked away stunned, but there were times when I heard something clearly as did others in these kinds of groups, and they wouldn't hear it.  I think the point is that if we can get the average Joe to hear at least some of them, we are on the right track.  And if the EVP is contextual, that's even better.  For example, I asked them to say 'hi' to my friends by name, and they did, albeit Class B.  These people were in shock.  Vicki
