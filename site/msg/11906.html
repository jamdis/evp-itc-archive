It's called "evidence." Heard of it? All this talk about the Fish  
Group is predicated on things that happened to other people. Ever  
played Chinese Telephones? Without the original EVP files, then its  
all a case of third-fourth-eight-hand testimony, slowly blowing out  
of proportion and reaching legendary status before you can say "Bob's  
your uncle." If there never was any EVP evidence we'd not even be  
calling them the Fish Group. They gave that name to themselves,  
through EVPs. If you don't accept EVP as good--if not the best-- 
evidence we have of paranormal communication, why are you here?

Some of us here have trying to approach this research in a logical  
and basically scientific way for many years. I, a least, will not  
start rolling in imaginative speculation on anyone's whim.

Ann
