Hi Stefan,
 I'm attaching a very basic block diagram of the system. The initial tones are software generated. The software is called 'Audio Mulch'. The function to use is called '10 Harmonics'. I use a base frequency of 102hz. The waveform must be adjusted so the peaks are flat across the envelope, you will see what I mean when using the software, there is a free trial version of the software. The information on the SEC LED driver is available on Dr Stifflers web site. I do not have a link as the site pages seem to change frequently.
