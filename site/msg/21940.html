It's not that hard to build an external sweep for that model.  That will take away the clicking and also give you an adjustable sweep rate.

You basically just make a simple function generator that will give you a linear triangle wave.  It will connect to the 469 board to the "vt" pin, which is clearly marked.

The function generator kit can be bought here for $10

http://store.qkits.com/moreinfo.cfm/QK23

For an extra $5 they build it for you.
