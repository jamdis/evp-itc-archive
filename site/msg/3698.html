I did the simple hack on the RS 12-469. It doesn't have a speaker so I connected it to my stereo amp and speakers. The sound was then pickd up by my optical microphone and recorded to an Olympus recorder.

"We're thrilled that you will translate"

"Erland will make you gravity"

"You know what/why Heaven's a gate?"

"Tesla eliminated Tesla/extra filter"

"Now talk through....Grampa was missing Cedrick....Cedrick (louder)"

"We've been watching him, he never closes a flue" (I checked and the flue here was accidently left open)

Dave
