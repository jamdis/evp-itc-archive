Perhaps a compromise to these opinions is to consider that there are many devices, processes, and theories as to what works, what doesn't, what it does, and how it does it.

Here, I was focusing on one item (the box Frank has lent out to be tested), and the claim Frank has for the box:

"The Frank's box allows entities to use FM and AM radio broadcast as raw material, which these entities select and shape to communicate with the user.  The messages received are for the user, and rarely does anyone hear the same thing as the original listener."'

And with a proposed definition of entity as: "Intelligent communication from entities who are not living human beings".

Note this suggests a couple interesting tests, such as how rare or common is it for others to hear the same thing, and under what conditions.
