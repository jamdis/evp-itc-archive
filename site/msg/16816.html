energetic feeling I receive when connecting with someone who has truly

of souls), they return to Spirit and become the light & love they came
from.  Spirits have much better things to do than haunt us, they continue to
learn and evolve on the other side.  However, they do enjoy communicating
with us, especially letting their loved ones know they are ok.
