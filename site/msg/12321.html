Hi Carol.  I think the Ovilus and Puck are two completely different animals from any of the 
radio sweep units out there.  If anything, I'm thinking they'd be more similar to EVP maker 
with the allophones file.  I've always wondered at what point the manipulation by spirit 
takes place when using any of these methods.  

I have the Puck and haven't heard the name Seth so maybe its unique to the Ovilus.  I 
haven't worked with the Obilus (though I'd like to!) The Puck has a "phonemes" setting 
which allows it to pick combinations from 300 phonemes (speech fragments) so 
theoretically it should be able to produce many more words than what are on listed as 
choices when in "dictionary" mode.  The Ovilus also has a phonetic mode (according to the 
manual) but I don't know how many speech fragments it has to choose from.  Given that it 
does have the ability to combine fragments to form new words, as does the puck, it makes 
sense that people are getting words not the list.  

I only use the Puck in phonemes mode and have had amazing results with it during Q&A 
sessions.  When I lost my dog at the end of June, I asked the puck if it could tell me more 
about who she was with and within the next 30 seconds it responded, "Dylan, grandma .... 
pet that you love."  Dylan is the name of my grandson who was lost due to miscarriage, 
just a couple weeks before our dog died.  I find this astounding because A) the response is 
relevant, specific, and even included a phrase at the end, and B) this was created from only 
speech fragments, not words in dictionary mode.  

Margaret
