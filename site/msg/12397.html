I have noticed when doing any sort of paranormal research that it is 
always best to do so when in a calm or happy state of mind and not when 
you're upset or angry. Spirits pick up on this and it agitates them, from 
what I've noticed.

Frank gave out the boxes he made without any instructions for them, and as 
far as I can tell most people who received them learned from using them 
how to make them work. I think in this field that is the way to go about 
it. If someone has some tips, that's fine. I've picked up some tips and 
other things from other paranormal researchers, such as some suggestions 
on how to ask questions for EVP sessions and some recommended questions, 
and what not to do, but a lot of it boils down to common sense.

Zack
