Hi All;

I'm having instability problems that I think is caused by batteries, so I 
went to Batteries Plus yesterday and got the smallest 12 volt gel cells they 
had. I didn't want to charge the batteries connected a plus and minus 
supply, the plus side draws more current with the audio functions, so needs 
more charge current. I also didn't have the chips on hand to make a plus and 
minus adjustable supply for the charger. I came up with the switch shown to 
seperate the batteries for charging, or connect them in a plus and minus 
supply configuration to run the boards.
