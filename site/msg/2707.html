than others. Using no filter at all, at least on my computer echos 
too much..

where we hear what it's saying over and over until it dies down -- 
then the next thing it says does the same thing. Attached in one of 
my evp's I recorded when using this software. As you see, the spirit 
is talking but that voice is echoing a lot. The first part says "It's 
Linda"... followed by 'cigerette' over and over too. I was holding up 
a cigerette and asking for anyone to tell me what it is. They 
answered that question. 

-- The save feature is disabled on the trial versions of this 
software but have been able to get around that by opening up cool 
edit, clicking on record and then switching back to DC Live and 
clicking Live Preview. Whatever was coming over the DC Live preview

the speaker for it to record but not close enough to cause any 
feedback. But as you know, that feedback problem is there with DC 
Live anyway but because I have a volume control on my speaker, I can 
adjust the volume to keep it from going into a screeching high pitch.
