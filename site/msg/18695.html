The sweep for the TRF is the same as the regular linear sweep for the
other boxes. On the hack radios you could just cut the VT wire and insert
the sweep voltage. I do that to other radios, find the tuning line with a
volt meter or scope, try to trace it back to it's source than interrupt it
and insert the external sweep voltage. You can use a pot and a 9 volt
battery for manual sweep. MK484=TA7642 from electronics123.com
