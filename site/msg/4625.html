Hi Ursula and EVP-ITC community,

I'm new to this group and read with great interest the experiments 
that you've all been involved in.  I agree totally that the 
individual's heart and mind are the most important tools in the 
capturing of EVPs and/or communicating with other dimensions in that 
from my limited personal experience and the observation of others in 
this field, I believe the messenger is just as important as the 
message being conveyed.  In other words, I've observed that in most 
cases, especially in those cases where a group of people are at a 
supposedly haunted locale trying to capture EVP with DVRs, that 
either only one person receives a message to a question or the 
messages received by everyone in the group is different.  This leads 
me to believe that the individual is just as an important a 
channel/receiver as the equipment being employed.  Therefore, I feel 
one must endeavor in one's personal life to strive towards all things 
positive as the laws of attraction apply in that you'll get back what 
you put out (to put in crudely).
