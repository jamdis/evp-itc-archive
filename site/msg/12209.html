Welcome to the group. 

Please use mp3 format to post audio files. And definitely small clips here. If you wish to post wav files, entire sessions,  or larger files use http://www.esnips.com to post and share with the group. They give you 50 GB free so you can post as much as you wish and share the links. It even has a nifty counter to let you know how many visited your files and a place they can leave comments.

Files that are large will ultimately be removed to make room for the group. Any and all files posted (even small and correct formats) are subject to removal when/if space becomes an issue. If everyone works together on corrcting this then it wont be a problem =)

Over 400 people in the group, only 100 megs of space... the math on that is pretty simple. So we use other avenues for posting files such as attaching it to an email post or putting it on esnips or putfile etcetera.

For those who need it you can use the free online file convertor at 

http://media-convert.com/
