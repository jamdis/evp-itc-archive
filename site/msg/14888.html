The running theory is this: The entity only needs some complex noise source (whatever that may be) in or to modulate or demodulate (change) the signal to form communication. A lot of the radios are used (Frank's box, shack hacks, minibox or whatever) because they put out a complex noise signal with a lot of human voice tones in them. It is thought that it would be easier for them to manipulate the voice tones than to try to manipulate random noise.

I personally use silence to record, which really isn't silence at all but just ambient room noise. The signal is run through certain filters and I get pretty good solid speech. I have worked with different setups such as the hacks and the like but prefer the more basic forms that, to me are more reliable and less questionable.

No matter what you decide to use to record they will all work to some extent in the end. All you need to communicate is the will, desire and an interested entity around you.
