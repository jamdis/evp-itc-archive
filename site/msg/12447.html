I have found it is effective to let participants hear their own
message even if it's a group setting. I wouldn't dream of interpreting
Box communication for other people. However, we have had sessions
where everyone quietly scribed what they heard and compared notes with
each other after closing the session. We also agreed before hand what
questions would be asked and have had sessions where no questions are
asked. -Bill
