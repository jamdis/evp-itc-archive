Hi Misty been a long time since we spoke.
 
I remember back early this year when I first met Frank, and he too made the same comment as you, that you need not say anything.
 
I on the other hand could not accept this concept, but now after many of my crime case recordings, and reviewing all my questions asked, the voices told me what I needed to hear to get the right leads. As soon as I turn that radio on ,they start talking about what happened, and even when i am asking them a question they are still talking. I have had to learn how to block out my questioning to hear what they said when I was talking. 
 
 I did try a recording without asking a question once, but just could not do it again as I have this in my head you need to ask questions.
 
Its hard to let go of, but you are right, they know what you need to hear.
 
Mike
