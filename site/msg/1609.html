Hi Zack, I have a new radionics kit based on the original Hieronymus
machine. It made from Bakelite, even the stick plate is. I still need
to wire it up so I just placed the components on the still unassembled
board and uploaded a pic in my photo album on this forum. The stick
plate is also made of Bakelite but I placed a small plastic square
where the stick pad goes. My crazy plane was to adapt it to the tuning
mechanism of Frank's Box to allow an intuitive tactile feedback
interface so I could try to find the frequencies where spirits contact
us. 

Sort of like the manual linear sweep but with a stick pad added. These
3 dial machines are still available from Bill Jensen out of Knoxville
TN who makes them to original spec except with modern internal
circuitry. -Bill Murphy
