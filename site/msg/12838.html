So, what's up with the frequency 1485 kHz? I've already mentioned 
that I'm reading the book, 'Voice Transmissions With The Deceased', 
by Friedrich Jrgenson. He discovered he could receive EVPs in the 
late 1950s during a recording session where he was capturing bird 
calls. 

In the spring of 1960 one of the voices he captured on tape told 
Jurgenson to "use the radio" as a medium. After that, he used this 
radio technique until his death. He connected a microphone and a 
radio receiver to the tape recorder and in this way he could have a 
real-time conversation with his "friends", as he called them. 

Usually he would set the radio for reception at frequencies where 
there is generally a variation of noises coming through. Later, he 
maintained the receiving frequencies to around 1445-1500 kHz. 1485.0 
kHz has been labelled the Jrgenson Frequency. 

Another very interesting tidbit is; In 1978 Jurgenson held a press 
conference and a number of lectures for groups. It was then that he 
predicted that we would soon be able to receive messages through 
television as well. He labeled the work "Audioscopic Research." 

As you can see today, many use this form of ITC in an attempt to 
receive images of entities or spirits on a monitor or television set. 
There have even been a number of movies about this form of spirit 
communication.
