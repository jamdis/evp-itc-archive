I never had alot of luck with it either. I tried it before with group many times but it was so distant and never clear or just nothing at all. But every now and then when I do a manual sweep I notice while I sit on dead air that sometimes thats where the best evp would come thru so I started to make it a habit that when I start and stop my sweep sessions its off station in the dead zone. I was doing a sweep the other night and they came thru real clear there with messages for my friend so I thought hmmm lets try just DRV and no sweeping and see if they can connect or if I can hear them. I heard nothing live just buzzing noise or what sounds like air/waves/whooshing now and then. So its not like some get where they hear it loud and clear and have conversations back and forth. At least not yet.

Maybe a few adjustments could change that I dont know. I never know why some days they are clearer than others.
