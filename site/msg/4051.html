Want to make a completely portable Ghost Box for under $100?

In these videos I share a very practical way to use the 12-470 Radio 
Shack Hack device.
This one is a totally portable system and uses a noise generator, the 
hacked 12-470, a digital (or analog) recorder, and a microphone which 
gathers the raw audio sweep, white noise (or in my case a special 
white/pink noise file I created), and any ambient noise within a 
chamber.. and places that session into a .wav file.
It also includes an ear piece (or speaker) so you can hear, real time,
what the digital recorder is recording (the mixture of the session 
sound within the chamber).
