Robin-- I have watched this clip and it is the least convincing of  
all the ones I have seen of him with the box. I have heard several  
where some of the audio is clear enough to stand on its own without  
his interpretation, and where his interpretation generally agrees. I  
have also heard some where the audio I hear disagrees with his  
interpretation, but not to such a high degree that I was overtly  
troubled by it--it was in what I would call the high-end of my  
tolerance range for subjectivity.  I have heard audio where the  
person asking questions of the box clearly heard answered that  
appeared to move them greatly without dependance on Chris's  
interpretation.

So, to try to sum up for you, my personal investigation led me to  
believe that something was happening with the box. I did not always  
agree with Chris's interpretation, but it appeared the same or close  
to what I heard a high enough percentage of the time that I was  
willing to accept he was not faking it, but giving it an honest  
enough go. It led me to start hounding Frank for a box of my own, and  
later to buy a minibox from Rick, since poor Frank was beset on all  
sides. ;)

We've already had some spectacularly burning arguments about what  
Chris claims his guides or technicians tell him behind closed doors.  
As far as I know, no audio of this is public, so his claims can't be  
evaluated. I think the proliferation of people getting results with  
the boxes belies his assertion that only 30 are chosen.
