I also listened to Steve H.'s 2 hour recording from Eastern State  
Penn last night. I found some of it amazing and some of it wildly  
annoying, especially the researcher close by the microphone who was  
chewing gum. That aside, there was more very odd and wonderful  
communication, and also a lot of Chris seeing shadows, apparitions,  
black masses crawling down hallways, and other things that no one  
else seemed to see. I wanted to ask Steve H. if he had witnessed any  
of this, since he was with Chris the whole time. For the listener,  
the effect was that of a veritable mob of live humans who (the posse  
that night) who were kept in a constant state of anxiety. They didn't  
need the extra hype, HMO, 'cause the pissed off dead inmates were  
being surly enough on their own.

I'd say I agreed with Chris's interpretations--and granted, I was not  
there and did have problems hearing because of gum smacking and other  
people rustling and talking--about 30 percent of the time. That said,  
I heard a lot of things loud and clear.
