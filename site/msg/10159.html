The only "meaningful" message I could get in a 1 minute session was
"I am".

Since morse codes are precise, there is not much room for variations
like with the allophones which are "decoded" by our flexible hearing, so
the gain of meaningful messages will be much less here. But nevertheless
I think it's worth to experiment with it.
