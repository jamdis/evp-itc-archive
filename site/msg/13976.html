Hi Susan.  I don't know what kind of box you'll end up with, but in the meantime, you can always do "manual radio sweep."  I get some of my best results that way.  When I started doing sweep for EVP, commercially available boxes were not available.  My favorite unit so far is the Duracell KP028 crank flashlight/radio.  It might be listed as the "3 in 1" or something like that.  The knob is just the right size and tightness to make it easy to sweep from one end to the other.  Of course, it's a little obnoxious to have to stop and crank it up every few minutes, but I think the results make it well worth the effort.  I've seen others have good success with all kinds of different radios though.  So many makes/models can work well.

I own the Paranormal Systems units and many of the Radio Shack hacks, and by comparison, this little $20 model really holds it's own.  PLUS it would be handy in case of power outage.  I think it's always great to have items around the house that can fulfill more than one purpose.  And so budget friendly too.  ;-)

Just a thought.....
