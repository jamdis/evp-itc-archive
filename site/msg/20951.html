Rule #1 of being an EE: Always get the data sheet. Most manufacturers
have the data sheets available free for download as pdf files these
days. Back in the old days we had to request them and wait for several
weeks until they arrived in the mail.

You can do a Google search for a part number and it will usually come
up with a link for the data sheet unless it's a really old, obscure
part by a manufacturer that isn't around anymore.
