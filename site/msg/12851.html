When I got my Novice license in 1967 I mainly hung out on 40 and 15 
meters.  Didn't have an antenna that worked on 80 meters (or so I 
thought). I've since learned that any piece of wire can be tuned to 80 
meters and some of them actually radiate!
:-)

I'm curious about this 1485 kHz frequency.  And I was surprised the FCC 
website I listed would come up with so many foreign stations on 1480 and 
1490. Lots of interesting data from that website.  Trans World Radio in 
Bonaire on 800 kHz runs 500 kW - that's half a million watts!
