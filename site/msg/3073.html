I truly believe that the resonant condition will not allow the success
of mass production of this device. I think we are going to come to the
conclusion that the human component is far more important then the
other components, although they all work together. 
We have all heard others stating their own inability to "make the box
work". These statements have been followed by claims of fraud and
deception. 
I suppose that I should not concern myself with that. I know that the
device works and I doubt that anything could convince me otherwise.
Yet, I remain concerned. I am concerned for those who have been
successful. Right now, we have few that have been willing and able to
build the device themselves, hence few that have been able to claim
deception. What happens to the credibility of the few when many are
unsuccessful? Should that concern me? I am extremely passionate about
this field, sometimes to a fault I would suppose. 
One brief glance around this community shows the fragile egos at work
all clamoring to move up some invisible ladder via whatever means
possible. There is little support amongst investigators and
researchers. Lines have already been drawn as a result of this device.
I am not confidant in my psychic abilities at all but I feel pretty
sure that there is going to be some major fall out as a result of this
mini-box being sold to whoever wants it. I hate the "wait and see"
approach. I'm too much of a control freak! LOL
