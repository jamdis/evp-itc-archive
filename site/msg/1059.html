Yes, the speaker is about a foot from the lamp/phototransistor combo. Volume has to be adjusted so the mic does not overload, it is a touchy adjustment of audio and flame level. Also the room should be dark and computer monitor turned down to reduce noise the mic will pick up produced stray light sources.
