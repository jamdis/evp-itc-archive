The Faraday cage mentioned in the book was referring to the Raudive 
diode method. He wanted to make sure to eliminate the possibility of 
RF being detected by his diode input.
It worked and he still received EVPs.

I just wanted to try it with the RSH knowing that it would eliminate 
the RF that makes the RSH work. Since we don't know for sure what is 
the best way, all ways are interesting and fun to try.
Since the RSH is still sweeping each frequency, you never know what 
might be transmitted in that range, even though it's not receiving my 
local stations... maybe something else is on a particular frequency.
