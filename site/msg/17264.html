I have had that thought and have had a couple instances where that might be
true. But I have a lot of evidence that contradicts that, too.

I like to make recordings in all sorts of places, some supposedly haunted,
some not supposedly haunted, some have no reason to be haunted (like a
brand-new shopping mall) and the responses or lack of them is totally
unpredictable.

I've been in several of the supposedly most haunted cemeteries in Illinois
many times, day and night, and recorded several hours of audio in them,
asking questions that might get an EVP response. In some I got nothing.
Other cemeteries that were supposedly haunted got some responses. A lot of
other places (old and new buildings, etc.) also seemed to get a few EVP
responses occasionally and at other times nothing.

So all I can say is that what happens, happens. I can't predict it. This
observer in particular doesn't seem to have a lot to do with the presence or
lack of responses and what those responses will be.
Zack
