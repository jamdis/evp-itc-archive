I strongly recommend that if you're serious about EVP work - you 
should get a copy of the English translation of Konstantin Raudive's 
first book "Breakthrough: An Amazing Experiment With Electronic 
Communication With The Dead" and D.J. Ellis' book "The Mediumship of 
the Tape Recorder".

Both can be found on the Amazon UK web site and copies are still 
available even though Ellis' book is long out of print.

Raudive's book:

http://www.amazon.co.uk/Breakthrough-Amazing-Experiment-Electronic-
Communication/dp/0900675543/ref=sr_1_1/203-4923892-9954337?
ie=UTF8&s=books&qid=1172891158&sr=1-1

Ellis' book: 

http://www.amazon.co.uk/Mediumship-Tape-Recorder-David-
Ellis/dp/095060240X/ref=sr_1_1/203-4923892-9954337?
ie=UTF8&s=books&qid=1172891351&sr=1-1

I bought my copy of Ellis' book there over 6 months ago (I found a 
paperback copy of "Breakthrough" at a local used 
bookstore)...Amazon.co.uk took my credit card easily, made the 
proper currency exchange (I usually check first with an online 
currency exchange calculator), and shipped it via e-mail. Got the 
book within a few days!

You can also find used and difficult-to-find books at ABE Books 
(these 2 are listed at the moment):

http://www.abebooks.com

The Internet is a godsend for those of us who need items only 
available elsewhere in the world and so are credit cards as far as 
making payments easier.

download versions): "EVP And New Dimensions" and "The Sounds of
