Ron,

I visited Mayan ruins in the Progresso/Merida region about 5 years 
ago...fascinating to me and very spiritual.

I just want to encourage you.  Set-up a certain time to run sessions 
weekly.  Let's say you could do it Monday -Thursday at 10:00 AM for 
example.  This way, an expectation is set and a plan is in order.  
Keep recording small sessions and then analyze them.  Ask questions, 
simple basic questions for responses each day.  Don't give up.  I 
pesonally have noticed this has helped me and something is building 
with the contacts and quality of the messages.  For me it began with 
a staggered "Yes," and other one words occasionally.  Now I am 
hearing sentences and very intelligable responses that I know are not 
radio bit coincidences.  The biggest help any of us can give you is 
to remain patient and continue experimenting, looking for not only 
what works for you, but giving those on the other side time to find 
you and get a message or two across.  It will come - I am certain of 
it.

For some reason, the amount of audio bits seem to help with the 
communication, as I can tell...even when I have received EVP-like 
messages above or over the audio bits in the background.  I do not 
understand why.  Here are two ideas I have for you to pick up more AM 
audio:

1) Try an AM antenna booster (Radio Shack sells them.  I think Juli 
has had some success with hers).
2) You may try more than one hacked radio sweeping at different 
places on the band.  Pipe the audio through a mixer, and output it 
through amplified speakers or directly into the recorder.  This way, 
you wil have audio bits throughout - even if you have to use 3 radios 
to do it.
