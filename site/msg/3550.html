I say to you clearly, now, that Chris Moon is free to do whatever the

from experimenting, too. I have formed an opinion that he is inclined  
to control the message by reading his own words and hearing his own  
words on the radio and on tapes of his sessions, and I cannot  
logically interpret them in any other way. Since you don't agree, If  
you would like to directly quote him to prove otherwise, then I am  
always ready to modify my opinions. Truly. But I am not going to  
change my mind without evidence.

I don't pull my opinions out of a void. I have seen what are, at this  
point, *generations* of paranormal researchers try to control the  
message and the process by saying that only the Special get to be  
part of the circle or the gang or the team or the bridge, or  
whatever. I have seen every single one of these units explode or  
implode or turn into a nascent religion over the control issues of a  
dominant person. I do not like this messianic behavior because it has  
ruined what was otherwise a potentially profitable avenue of  
research. Because I don't talk about my own history or abilities  
often, and because I don't yet have a box, that does not mean that I  
am not a hoary old raven with a hell of a lot of perspective on this.  
I've watched it happen again and again and again--thank goodness,  
mostly from a distance.
