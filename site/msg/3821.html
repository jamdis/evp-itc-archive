pretty clearly. It was quite intense and I listened to one file after  
the other, without stopping, because I was so interested. But I still  
didn't hear a lot of what Chris "interpreted." I did, however, hear a  
lot of things he did not comment on at all. For example, when the  
discussion was centered around the woman who kept claiming that she  
hid there and the guards brought her there, the historian from the  
prison said that the building you were in post-dated women being  
there.  I clearly heard the woman loudly respond by calling the  
historian "bitch" and "c**t." I also clearly heard--and someone else  
onsite remarked on it--a clear radio fragment ending with "dot.com!"

this wouldn't be a big deal, except that I got the idea that Chris's  
box was somehow vaccinated against this occurring. ;)

But here's my question to you: Did you see *any* of the shadows,  
apparitions, or crawling black shapes? Because what I heard was a lot  
of Chris going, "Did you see that???!!! A black shadow just went

Huh?" So, you personally, an eye witness?
