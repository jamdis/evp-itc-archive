Well, easy if you know what you're looking for. You have to find the tuning
voltage line coming form the processor, then cut it and insert your own
tuning voltage. I didn't even know about the mute function when I did these,
I use a different conversion method.

 I have an older, made in 1998 Radio Shack home stereo that I got out of the
trash at work, that had the service manual wiht it, so I found the mute
circuit and removed the two transistors to make it sweep. Then added a push
button so the tuning button doesn't have to be held in. Push the button in
and instant GB mode. That one collects duct too, but it's heavy.
