Keith at ITC Bridge sent a memo stating Debbie Caruso passed last night. Some of you may know of Debbie's work through the AA-EVP or her many tv appearances, or perhaps her website http://www.1800heaven.com/aboutme.htm

She was one of the 1st people that I met that seemed to be an evp magnet. She made a real contribution to the field and her work will always be appreciated.
