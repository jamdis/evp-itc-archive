Each switch wafer has three segments, so there's only two wafers in the  
battery switch. Using the switch simplifies the chargers circuit, so I don't 
need plus and minus charger supplies, all three charge regulators being run 
from a single transformer and DC supply. The switch being out of the "junk 
box", so cost-less. I seem to have collected exactly what I needed over the 
years, and I always seem to have exactly what I need. Number 21 has a 
similar switch.
