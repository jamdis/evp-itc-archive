First off, there are some diagrams, I believe, in the Files or Photos
sections of some of Frank's boxes. There's really not a lot to it. Get
a radio with electronic tuning capability and build a sweep circuit.
Frank just posted a circuit for a sweep the other day. You could even
do manual tuning with an analog radio by tuning the knob up and down.

Why do you need to record above 20 kHz? You can't hear it. No one that
I know of has captured any EVP or ITC even close to that range. Most
of my EVP have been in the lower normal human voice range.
