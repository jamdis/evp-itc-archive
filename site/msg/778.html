I kept getting this nmessage for years, thinking it was the echo box they 
wanted open. I tried that a few times, and it made no difference. It turns 
out that if I leave the front panel of the receiver open, the feedback 
between the echo box mic, and the monitor speaker is almost eliminated. On 
one of the mini boxes I drilled 3/8 holes in the cover to cut out feedback, 
in a recent system I raised the front panel on 1/2" stand offs and that 
works. So I do get some guidence from the "guys", I just don't understand 
it.......most of the time!! Curiously, when I first got that message I heard 
"Frank has a girl friend", which was false, then it seemingly changed to 
"leave the box open" both having the same amount of syllables. That was back 
in 2003. It really makes one question one's hearing!
