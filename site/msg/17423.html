How much do you trust your own observations? How certain are you of what you
see and hear and don't see and hear? I think it comes down to that.

People with a lot of uncertainty may claim things like "pareidolia" or
whatever, but if you have the certainty and confidence to say you hear this
or that, then you really don't care.

No doubt some people DO fall pray to pareidolia (whatever that is) but I'm
not one of them. I do have to wonder about the people who see "orbs" in
every photo but don't know enough about photography to say whether they may
in fact be something uusual or not. I'm not one of them.

I think it's a good idea to put the audio clips up here for others to listen
to. Others may hear something different. That doesn't mean you're wrong in
your initial interpretation. You're free to agree with the other person and
revise what you say you hear. And you're free not to.

Does this make sense or do I need more coffee this morning?
