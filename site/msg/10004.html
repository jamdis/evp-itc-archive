I think 10 seconds is about right, IMHO, but any shorter and you'll 
find yourself speaking over replies sometimes (even at 10 seconds).  

Formerly being a stickler against editing, I now do edit some.  For 
example, if I have a 5 second clip and there are some scrub noises 
between words that interfere with the message, I now cut it from the 
clip.  This makes it easier for first timers to be able to identify 
and comprehend the "manipulation" and how it sounds...allowing them 
to hear what to listen for in responses.

If you are going to cut/edit, just keep the raw audio as 
backup...proof for those who question, if you will.
