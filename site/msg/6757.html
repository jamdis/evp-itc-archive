Ron,

When I ran a group (5 of us) session two weeks ago, I had members of 
the group formulate questions and I did the asking for about an 8 
minute session or so.  They could not hear the responses aloud, as I 
had the audio piped directly into a mixer, then outputted to the 
recorder (but I could hear & had on headphones to listen in).

I immediately then uploaded the audio to my laptop, and began playing 
the audio aloud, allowing the group to help me analyze what we had 
captured.  We received great messages that were not lower level, but 
sentences of information (not just "hi," "yes," "no," etc).  

So, I would like to add to this discussion that group sessions can 
work well, but it may depend on how you do it and what the intentions 
are.

LC
