The only way I know to accurately find EVP's in a recording is to find
a quiet room to listen to the recording, put on some good comfy
headphones and listen to it.  I keep a notepad handy to take notes. 
After all audio has been reviewed, I go back and cut out the bits I
want to keep.  If I have 8 hours of audio on an investigation, it
takes me at least 2 to 3 times that to accurately process that audio.
 There is no other good way that I know of.
