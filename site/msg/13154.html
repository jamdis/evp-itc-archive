know who Art Bell is. He was one of the first to openly talk on the

Art Bell's mother passed away - Jane Lee Bell

had died on 23 December 2008.

attending and watching the induction of Art Bell into the Radio Hall of 
Fame, last month. She congratulated her son in a video taped message.
