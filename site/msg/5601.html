My feelings are that if we are communicating with spirits, and if 
they are able to bring forth ghosts (earthbound spirits), our duty 
would be to help them to transition unto the light.  Therefore, 
whatever messages they need to bring to the earth realm, or whatever 
messages we need to give them, that will free them and allow them to 
move beyond "here" should be the goal.  Again, I say all this as an 
IF...call it spirit rescue, etc.

I would agree then that fear must be vanquished to be able to deal 
with such things.  I told one person that "I am the light," and she 
couldn't understand that statement.  Perhaps we simply need to "know" 
who we truly are and the power we have, rather than surrendering it 
over to someone or something else through fear.

I post all this, but again will say, "whatever works for each 
individual I am OK with."  We are all on different paths and at 
different places in our spiritual journeys.  We all have different 
ideas, theories and beliefs. I can surely extend grace to all, as I 
can empathize with the masses and their place in this world.
