I agree with you about the "not completely conscious" statement (in my humble opinion). Let's just for a moment assume we are dealing with dead people:  if a person were to somehow get stuck in a bad state (something similar to the state where weird, frightening dreams are made), he might predict some frightening, strange stuff -- not out of maliciousness, but because he doesn't know better.
