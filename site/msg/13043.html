building facing and told me to get it to a psychic. At the time I

was well before I had my awakening interest in the paranormal or
spirit communication. As a child, basically, just out of high school I
tucked the piece away until after moving countless times, I lost it. A
shame, as the majority of the rubble lies under a hundred feet of soil
at the bottom of the eastern oklahoma county landfill now and a
special opportunity was lost to youthful ignorance.

anytime, but it would certainly make sense that an object or location

communication a lot easier. Just my thoughts, not backed up with any
conclusive date for or against, but I always found session
communications seem easier (as far as results) at location of the
event. Then again the voices I received did not correlate with the
event. I am very interested in hearing what happens after your
sessions, please keep us updated.
