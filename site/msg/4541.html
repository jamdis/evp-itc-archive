As most of us know, Sarah Estep, was the founder of the AA-EVP--the  
route by I, and many of us, first got to the place we're at today.  
I've heard from a friend of mine who is also of that generation of  
EVP tapers, that Sarah's condition is deteriorating. I'd like to ask  
all of you to send positive thoughts her way, as she was always a  
very gracious lady who was more than helpful to many, many people who  
were interested in taping. I bought my first equipment from Sarah's  
husband and benefited greatly from her wisdom, always given freely.  
She will be remembered as giant in this field.

Please think of her kindly this Christmas Eve.
