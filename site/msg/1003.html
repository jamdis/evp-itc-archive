a tesla "coil" is a radiofrequency step-up transformer in which the 
primary and secondary are each tuned to the same frequency.

the above dictates that primary be high C  low L and the secondary be 
highL and lowC

the primary and secondary dont need to be co wound; they can be 
several feet apart---thats how tesla invented radio; he just kept 
moving the primary and secondary farther and farther apart.

it is intrinsic to a high C tank circuit that it is easily shock 
excited and will resonate on harmonics.  a high L circuit is the 
reverse.


the high L tries to "re-sinerize" the carrier and construct the 
modulation from noise provided by primary.

receiver should be near or connected to secondary and tuned to same 
frequency.

BOTTOM LINE:

amplifier, handheld radio etc must be operated on the lowest voltage 
which it will function on; 4 volts not nine volts.  thats behind the 
ubiquitous  observation "i just started getting voices when batt 
died.  with new batt, no voices.  spirits must be angry or something."

without voltage starvation in receiver/amplifier you mainly amplify 
the noise and not the desired residue of subquantic voice bolstered 
by cohered noise.
