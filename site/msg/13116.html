Yes, that's right.  Most commercial FM and TV antennas are collinear 
arrays which have quite a bit of gain.  AM antennas usually use phased 
verticals at particular spacings to produce a pattern, but the Fresnel 
zone is very large for such frequencies, so if you're close to them, the 
Fraunhofer zone pattern isn't valid.
