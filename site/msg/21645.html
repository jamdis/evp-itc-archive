Most of what we are doing right now, in this early stage, requires one on one, face to face conversations I believe. And we really enjoy the challenge of interactive question & answer communication about our work.

April is one of the paranormal investigators and ITC researchers we have testing equipment for us, and yes there are others, as well as scientists in other labs in controlled settings as we are collecting data to publish results. 

The device we are focusing  on right now outside of the ITC probe is one that Andy developed  based on probe results which derived from a paranormal investigation involving an autistic child that was noncommunicative. What happened that night gave him an idea about how to reach other autustic children like this one. Its basically the probe reverse engineered. It has been approved for and currently in an official "trial" being used by select schools where teachers & families are using it with fantastic success to communicate ...and is based on electromagnetic signals.

On a side note.....that device is unexpectedly working for us in the field in paranormal experiments, in much the same way that the ITC probe is, we now know. We've been taking it out over the last few weeks and excited that its allowing people to hear and converse in much the same way as the probe....only I believe much easier and much more clearly.

These devices are in the very, very early stages of development and we havent thrown a great deal of information or recordings out there yet since we are still in gathering data mode, trying to answer some specific questions of our own and hoping to publish what we've discovered in as responsible a way as possible. 

We are gathering a pretty strong support group in the scientific community, which is tough given the subject matter, but the results some guys are seeing and experiencing themselves is creating quite a wave rumbling through the community. The people backing us up and supporting us are a really cool preview of what is to come and we are stoked. There will always be a few outraged chest beaters who wont like what is happening, but it will be very difficult for them to argue with the math and the data.
