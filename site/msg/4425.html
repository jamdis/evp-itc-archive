Oh, Bruce...I agree.  

The stuff I have been working on outside this ghost box stuff was 
completely led by "spirits" on the other side.  My job, if you will, 
was to begin a website and build it for the sole purpose of getting 
others to consider that there might be more than the physical realm.  
That led to Angels & Ghosts in 2004, and I had no idea why that name 
was given, but I went with it.  I love the tech side, but have felt 
the goal of A & G was to make people think and decide for themselves. 
There are plenty of the debunkers, TAPS type investigators out there, 
and that was not the vision I had been given (though I like that 
approach).  We recieve submissions from all over the world of audio, 
pictures, videos, stories, etc, and then I post much if it monthly.  
Again, the site's flavor is "you decide."  In the digital age, there 
is no way to authenticate what's absolutely truth, from fiction.  So, 
again my mantra has been, "You decide."  Do I think everything on A & 
G is of ghosts and spirits?  Nah...not my job to anyway.  I make it 
interesting, and allow the minds of the visitors to decide.  That's 
been my job and the spirits' beckoning.  They want people to begin 
looking for more than what the five sense can bring them.  I feel 
they speak often, but we don't listen enough.  However, I also feel 
they speak often and we think it is coincidence or better yet our own 
thoughts - LOL. ;)  

January of '07 began with a dream and a "new technology" I was to 
work on that would be big.  I sort of blew it off as a pizza dream, 
but then was led to contact people from my past for "other purposes" 
of business  This of course unwittingly led back to this whole 
communication box, again.  They (spirits) will not let up on me.

So I am working on projects and I get nudged in the spirit to get on 
this box thing again.  I pop on here, see the latest news with the RS 
Hack (they bugged me as soon as it broke, but I stalled a few days) 
and am now running experiments, etc with it.  I am posting articles, 
videos and audio about it to spread the word.  More to come...
