Predictions from Box is really what drew me in to this particular method of ITC. A Franks Box built in March 2007 predicted a very specific military stike. I was hesitant to post the information at the time since it seemed inflammatory but was shocked to see most of it come true. Here's the link to what happened and what Franks Box predicted to a large extent.

http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Operation_Orchard

I told Frank about this and then dialog with him about other forecast led to a interview with George Noorey about Doomsday Predictions. After the interview it was stated by a listener that people will look for events that fits the prediction and Box forecast were unreliable. The forecast that we spoke about concerned a catastrophe on the East Coast of the United States that may result in loss of life in a tidal wave from the Atlantic ocean and possibly due to a meteor strike. However, with no meteors being tracked and no huge storm in weather forecast bearing down on the easy coast, it was a dicey prediction as it was August 14 and the prediction said the danger would be on the 15th but no year was given. Here's the link to that broadcast:

http://www.coasttocoastam.com/show/2009/08/14

At that point Frank and I knew at least we went public with information regarding a disaster and we provided specific information. Then that next day, August 15, a sudden storm developed into Tropical Depression Bill, intensified to Tropical Storm Bill, and formed a large eye. Within days, a newly dubbed Hurricane Bill would end the life of a 7-year-old girl who drowned after she, her father, and a 12-year-old girl were swept off a rocky ledge near Acadia National Park in Maine. Here's the link to info on that storm:

http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hurricane_Bill_(2009)
