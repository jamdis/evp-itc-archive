I haven't experienced the EVP/ITC, but many cemeteries have guardians that 
try to ward you off in various ways.  From my experience, if they finally 
decide you're not there to do harm they will kind of back off and leave 
you alone.

A simple thing like straightening a skewed tombstone or cleaning up a mess 
will convince them you're not there to harm. Also, tell them that you're 
not there to do damage.
