Even the older '470 I have uses digital pulses on the VT pin, so I 
wasn't able to sweep it it like 469. I haven't checked the other 
hack models yet. If anyone has these, and has a socpe, just look at 
the VT pin, if the radio has one, and see what it does when the 
radio is tuned.
