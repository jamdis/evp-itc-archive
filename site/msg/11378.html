There is NO research anywhere which shows that germanium diodes are 
superior to silicon diodes as far as the "diode method" is concerned.

Konstantin Raudive didn't pioneer the "diode method" (sorry - exactly 
who did escapes me for the moment). I have a copy of "Breakthrough" 
which is the only English translation of any of Raudive's books and 
the "diode method" is described in an appendix. The diagram in the 
appendix note shown there (and I can scan in the page in the book and 
post here, if anyone likes) shows a typical 1N914 diode which uses 
silicon as the base semiconductor.

The only "transcommunication device" I'm aware of that specifically 
called out for the use of germanium was the one diagrammed by the 
spirits in one of the Scole Experiments (and even then - it was a 
block of this material - not a component diode).

By the way - it was "Konstantin Raudive" (with a "k"), "Franz Seidl" 
(not Seidle...See Jean de Muelder's website for original diagrams of 
the Psychofon from Seidl's book 
http://jeandemeulder.transcommunicatie.nl/psychophone2.htm) 
and "George Meek" (no "s"). Additionally - George Meek's Metascience 
Foundation developed at least 5 different models of the Spiricom 
(most people are only familiar with Bill O'Neil's Mark IV).
