Hi Laura.  There's a patch cord available from radio shack for about $5.  You connect one 
end to the ear jack of the recorder and the other to the 'sound in' on the computer.  I use 
a freeware program called Audacity to record.  Just play the file into the computer (rather 
than doing an actual transfer).  Hope this helps some.
