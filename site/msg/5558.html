I've been with people who do protections and some who don't and my  
personal feeling is that it's not religion that matters so much as the  
intention of the person(s) reciting the words. It could be Catholic or  
Hebrew or Hindi or Wiccan, to me it's the good energy that's released  
for protection. A lot of psychics will try to send a white light for  
protection, and to me it's the same thing as prayer. I spent a little  
time with Chip Coffey at the Waverly in August, and he does protection  
before investigating plus others are known to do it after to tell  
spirits they are not welcome to hitch a ride back home with you.
