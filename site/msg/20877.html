No, they are made of catalin, which is vaguely similar to bakelite but is made by a different process. It could be colored in bright colors which was perfect for the Art Deco era. Catalin did not withstand the passage of time well and there haven't been any catalin radios made since around WWII. There aren't too many of those around any more. If you want to get an idea of what they are worth now, go to eBay and do a search for "catalin radio."
