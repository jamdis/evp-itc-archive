This is the 12-150 radio, it's simple as pie now that i did all the grunt work. Look at the picture, the fourth pin from the right is the AM. Right above it is the FM (C18). Notice that i cut the trace going to C18 in the picture, easily done with a razor. I used a jumper wire to connect AM and FM, the FM will not work unless you cut the trace going to it. The FM VT puts out 11.8 volts and the sweep circuit runs on 9V, that's where the problem was. You can figure that out easily with a scope or a multimeter. This box sounds pretty good right now, but the FM sweeps a little out of band. I can fix that.....but not today.
