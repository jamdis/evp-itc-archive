Aluminum seems to work the best. I got a voice that sounded like Raudive
saying copper. I have used plastic and wood. Different materials seem to
allow different levels, but it's hard to tell for sure who/what is talking.

 Folding the tube seems to make it act like a larger tube. With just a long
straight tube the sound tends to be tinny. I made a chamber 12 inches square
and 4 feet long from MDF board lined with aluminum foil years ago, the
voices were almost demonic sounding, so I took it apart.

 The only difference with the hacked radio is the sweep. With the hack radio
the sweep steps so the radio is tuned in 10khz increments. With the box it's
a continuous sweep, linear uses a triangle wave.
