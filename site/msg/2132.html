One of the reasons I believe the researcher is often part of the
process is because some investigators seem to be able to consistently
obtain evp's while others are not, despite many attempts over years of
investigating. Sometimes the teams in CA refer to those who often get
decent results as "evp magnets". 

I think the individuals who can easily obtain good results (no matter
where they are) may be able to channel their psychic abilities into PK
energy and record the results as an evp. I'm not suggesting this is
what happens every time but it may be what's happening in some of the
cases.
