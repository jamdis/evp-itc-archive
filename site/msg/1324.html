          A common, basic VHS VCR can be used as a low noise analog recorder. I supplied a crystal microphone and audio pre-amplifier and fed this into the 'audio in' on the VCR. Playback can be monitored on a TV set or the audio out can be fed to a computer. I post processed in ClearVoice to remove a slight 60hz hum and reduce the high frequencies. Background sound was provided by a high speed manual sweep tune of an FM broadcast radio. Here's a couple of the best so far.
