You would have to build what is called a "Black Hole antenna" in order to 
get uniform signal strength over the entire AM band.  This is from a 
design by John F. Sutton of NASA.  He holds a US patent, number 5296866. I 
also have the original papers he published on it.

No one commercially manufactures and sells a "Black Hole antenna" but many 
people have built them and are using them successfully.
