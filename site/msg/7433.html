Victoria,

Frank has no more idea how to "save" the legions of restless dead  
than the rest of us. We're trying to establish a clear two-way  
communication with those entities in other realms of life than our  
own. What you use the radio-sweep technology for is your choice, but  
because of the infancy of this research, it is best, in my opinion,  
to approach it without an agenda or preconceived notions.

I've also got to say that this may not be the area of research you  
want to commit to. You say you haven't slept well since the 1970s and  
that the Dead bother you. You don't sound like you have managed to  
develop a way of dealing with them that leads to less stress on you.  
This is very likely going to add to the stress, not relieve it.

There is inherent beauty in this research, but never come to it  
thinking that it is a panacea for either you or the "dead screaming  
for help." The reality is is that it's a hard, long slog for a few  
short messages. It's hours of sessions and reviews--a lot of which is  
very tiring, for reasons that may be natural and "supernatural."

Frank can't "do the world a favor." He's not hiding any big secret to  
life, the universe, and everything. No one here is. And anyone who  
claims otherwise is fooling both you and themselves.

Hard truths, but they need to be said.

Ann
