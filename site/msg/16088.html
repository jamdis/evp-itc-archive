In my mind the word "skeptic" is a misnomer. There is a difference between a
skeptic and an unbeliever.

If I said "I'm skeptical about your rebuilding that engine" I would admit
that it's possible, just doubt that you're going to do it.  An unbeliever
wouldn't believe that it's even possible to rebuild engines.

I think most of the "skeptics" we see in any sort of paranormal sphere are
really unbelievers. As Frank pointed out, they don't even believe or
recognize that we ARE spirits, so how could a ghost exist and how could the
box work? A ghost could hit them in the head with a 2X4 and they still
wouldn't believe it.

Zack
