I agree with you, Ursula, in that there is a fine line between 
objective and subjective reality and that one must somehow synthesize 
the two in digesting the information for oneself.  When exchanging 
findings with others, however, I'm always willing to give in that I 
respect those who see images I may not see, or hear voices I may not
