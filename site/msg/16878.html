Since most of the 2012 doomsday prophecies focus on the Mayan calendar, I thought I'd chimed in. I'm 1/2 Cherokee and have a PhD in Ethnic Studies with a specialization in Native American Studies. I know many Maya people and have studied their mythology and culture. I hate to break anyone's doomsday bubbles, but the Mayan calendar did not end with the date Dec. 21, 2012. That was the end date on one stellae. There are many others that go beyond that date, including one that goes well into the 4000s. Plus, there are many contemporary Maya daykeepers who continue working on the calendar. So, to those using the Mayan calendar as a prophetic compass, I really wouldn't worry  about the world coming to an end in 2012. Please don't take this post as pompous snottiness on my part. I see a lot of fear out there about 2012 and I'm just trying to alleviate that.

Pat
