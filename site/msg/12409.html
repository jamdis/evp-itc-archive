Hey Mike   Haven't talked to you for quite a bit since you sent me a 250.  By the way it is the best box for me so far. 
 
Mike, I have been at this EVP stuff for several months now and have had a limited degree of success due to my signal issues here.  And during these months I have been looking for a place or organization that might offer a workshop on equipment used for EVP.  I have never found a thing.
 
I happen to be the type of person that if I want to learn something I will attempt to get next to the expert and do hands-on-training if at all possible.  My fantasy is that people from this group (I won't name names now) and RTSC researchers and possibly others not in the two groups would get involved in organizing such a workshop.  I see a workshop where we all bring our equipment for group presentations and then split off into subgroups for practicing with an experienced EVPer.  An editing session would be critical as well.  I would see a final group meeting where actual clips from the day would be presented.   I think this would also be a good opportunity to work with non-evpers (such as mediums) to coordinate our communication efforts with the other side and to compare notes.
 
I bring this up to you now because of the continuing stuff I hear about this Moonie guy.  It seems to me that he is filling a void for people who cannot find a place to really work with learning about EVP technology.  I think and spirit phone should be in everyone's house...right next to their land line phone.    How can we get a workshop going with real researcher?    ron l
