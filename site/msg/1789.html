Bruce,

I actually listened to your files Bruce and they seem pretty clear 
that they are answering your questions.  The problem i've been 
fighting with is at what speed to set it at when it's automatic to 
have people not say it's just a radio DJ or part of a song.  I know 
the files are fast and are very fragmented, but there is no way to 
say it is the radio because of the speed.  You are correct in saying 
that it could be mistaken for words that our minds make up 
themselves.  I just post what i think i hear.  If i send it to 10 
people they probably would hear 10 different things or nothing at 
all.  That's the problem with any EVP, if you listen to the files 
enough no matter how you record them, you may think you hear one 
thing and convince yourself of it.  While doubters will always say 
you are nuts for hearing that.  There are no EVP experts 
unfortunately to sift through all our files and decipher them for 
us, so we just have to post what we have and let the general public 
come up with there own answers.  I will try and slow it down a bit 
and see if that does make a difference.  I never really thought to 
do that, but now that you suggested it, it seems like a good idea to 
go that direction next.  I think your manual sweep files are great 
captures, but there is really no right and wrong way to do EVP's in 
this experimental work.  It's whatever you have the best outcome 
doing i guess and stick with that.

-Steve
