What's happening is that= it is processing an area of the image, taking (for instance) the average v= alue of that entire area, and replacing all the blocks in that area with th= e average.  In video processing, this would result in a field of multi= -colored flowers being replaced by, for instance, a big greyish-bluish bloc= k, with no resemblance to flowers.  In the noise example, it maybe re= place all the random pixels with another big greyish-bluish block.  If= you did not know beforehand what the original picture was, you couldn't te= ll whether your pixelized image was really a field of flowers, a chevy, the= sky with clouds, or a random bunch of pixels.
