It does look to me like others are misunderstanding what Rasterer is saying.
This is a big problem with e-mail. I've never seen him state that the boxes
don't work.

I think it's rather admirable if someone has seen no evidence of themselves
over a considerable amount of time, yet keeps looking. Is that the case
here?

After a while, most people start to get discouraged. I have been in places
where others recorded EVP sessions and got some EVP, and my recorder a mere
inches away didn't get anything. Then there are times when I get dozens of
them and others get nothing. As I just noted in a previous post, there's an
awful lot we don't know here. My suspicion based on the above is that
whatever is producing the EVP can be highly selective. But I don't know that
for sure.

Zack
