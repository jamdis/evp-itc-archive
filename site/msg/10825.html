Yes, equipment differences (I noticed some earbuds do not pick up 
half of what headphones are good speakers do) and also what I would 
call a "syncopated rhythm" present with some of the messages.  That's 
my old music training coming out of me and used to describe a spacing 
between words spelling out a message, such as a word spoken every 
other second, etc - sounds abnormal in regular conversation but with 
some of these recordings it happens quite frequently.  Maybe this is 
due to the frequency changes, which also sometimes creates sort of a 
skip:  "They may (may) be able to (to) help me."
