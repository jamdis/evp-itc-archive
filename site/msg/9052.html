Hi Rees.  I think the most important thing is that the USE CUE LIST option be checked.  I 
like a little higher tone of voice, so I have my speed set at 106%.  And I have checked the 
box for play EVP 'continuously.'

When I listen back, if I want to slow the words a bit to increase clarity, I decrease the 
tempo by about -18.  Changing the tempo (rather than the speed) will slow the rate of 
speech without changing the pitch of the voice.  Decreasing speed will change both the 
rate of speech and lower the pitch of the voice.

Margaret
