> Al, this is the other Al, heh. I like the Olympus and have three of 
them and one Sony. IMHO the Olympus beats Sony hands down. Model WS-
300M is my favorite. I believe it runs 24-hours -- I use it for 
overnight evps in houses and have captured very clear sounds. It is 
stereo and snaps apart to convert into a memory stick complete with 
usb. You can store data/text files and music, and the tiny speaker is 
excellent. This thing is so light a good Cuban cigar weighs more. I 
believe these go for $150 or so, but I got this one new on ebay for 
$65. Another good one I use all the time is Olympus VN-960PC, which 
may not be in mfg. any more. It records about 16 hours and is 
slightly larger, but has a darn good mike and I like the clip for the 
shirt pocket. Also it has a lighted display (as does the ws-300m) 
thhat's larger than the other one, consequently with bigger figures 
for easier reading. You didn't ask for this, but the one I'd avoid is 
the Sony icd-st25. The sound is nowhere near true and though it has 
most of the features of the Olympus models the sound quality is first 
in my book. I think all of these are available at wally world, 
costco, and circuit city, though you can usually save a lot at ebay 
if you're patient.
