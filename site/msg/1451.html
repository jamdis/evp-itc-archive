Steve,

Here are some more questions that I have received EVP's on.

"Is there a reason why you stay in this room more than other rooms?"

"How old are you?"

"What city were you born in?"

"Were you ever married?"

"Would you like me to stay, or would you like me to go?"

"Please speak as loud as you can."

"How many children did you have?"

"How many of you are there?"
