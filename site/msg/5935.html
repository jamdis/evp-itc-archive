Babs,

I cannot answer many of your questions yet, as I am experimenting 
with different set-ups, methods, etc.  It is I who have a set-period 
of time set aside for these experiments...and with good reason.

My personal feelings are that there needs to be a commitment - 
something for the other side to rely upon with respect to me.  That 
expectation can then build to better audio responses-questions.  If 
we think we can spot shoot this like EVP and develop consistent, 
good, 2-way communication, I suspect we are in error.  I understand 
the same radio shows are going on at the same time, but I am looking 
beyond that.  It is my hope that once solid contact is proven, that I 
can move beyond my current set-times.  Personally, I want to hear 
intelligent replies that cannot be random chance.  Several of my 
recordings have convinced me, and they are in my files on this 
board.  Two of them I will mention specifically:

1) 11808womanghostvoice2.wav: The response I received from a woman's 
voice over top of the audio.  This was not radio jibberish, but more 
like an EVP.  The woman's voice was loud, and slowly annunciating the 
message for me to be able to hear it.

2) phonecallthatsbrucebruce11808.wav: I had asked a question about 
someone receiving a phone call.  In the audio, you can hear, "Phone 
call? Bruce. That's Bruce."  I ask, "Am I going to receive the phone 
call?" and the response is not a "yes" or "no," but again, "Bruce."  
That would be very hard to receive by chance, in my opinion.  You'll 
need headphones to hear it, but it's there.

Regarding the processing of audio, I am now moving to direct record 
with only trimming the audio segment out of the entire recording, no 
cuts, and only normalizing it to about 60db.  An example of this is 
the recording I made and uploaded this morning 
entitled, "spiritsspeakchuck12108.wav." 

Babs, you are going to have to experiment on your own and come to 
your own conclusions, I suspect.  That's what I am doing...trying to 
find what works for me and proves (to me) that I have broken 
through.  You sound frustrated with the results you have had so far, 
and I understand (especially with the 12-469). However, I do believe 
there is more than one person (or even 30 people) who will be able to 
do this consistently. Also, I would say that to believe only random 
vs linear sweeps of the band will work, does not remove any of the 
same possible errors you propose, really. ;)

In all, it's coming along I feel, but more experiments will need to 
be done.
