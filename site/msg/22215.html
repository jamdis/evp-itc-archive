I used the diagram of the web about the guy with the spiricom

I made a board that generated the 13 tones with sine or square wave output. Consisted of some MCUs and a mixer stage.
I modified some surplus ham equipment to send and receive across the 26-30 mhz range.

That was 2005-6 or so. I gave up shortly after starting. I did not give much thought about it at the time.  We had trouble with transmiters close to me, bleeding through most anything with an audio stage.The summer of 2007 it was shutdown. It was to late for me to try again. I had sold the 2 transceivers before they went away.

The good news, I can try evp recordings without all the interfernce.
