Regan:
             We originally Steve, myself and Jeff started doing sessions via Skye.  At that time I was not in position of a Frank's or Joe's Box.  Therefore we were able to conduct sessions using every ones boxes.  Upon receiving my #12 Franks Box, I want to afford the an opportunity to anyone that want to partake and experience all the boxes since they are not readily available.  This way everyone could learn the different pitches and tones for the different devices.  I figured I waited so long for mine and know how much it means to me, therefore, several of us didn't want those who didn't own one not to lose either, thus the Skye sessions were born (so to speak).  Even though you do not have the devices and/or boxes at your location, you can still record the session.
 
              I myself do 2 types of recordings and/or sessions.  One I just let the recorder run, like Frank does and the other I do ask questions.  What I am lead to believe either way, they already know what your pretty much going to ask so, sometimes its just better to NOT ASK QUESTIONS AT ALL, but to let them do their thing so you do not speak over them.
 
              Hope this helps!
