Good deal.  You should like the 125.  I've noticed the sweep on the AM is just slightly slower than the 469, but I've gotten the best communication with the 125.

Speakjet is an IC chip that will produce speech with the use of allophones.  The idea is that you'd ask questions like you would with the radio and a spirit makes use of the allophones to generate words.  The allophones act as the raw audio instead of radio snippets.  Someone in the group, can't remember who, brought this to our attention. 

Stefan Bion has taken the idea of allophones and incorporated it into his software program EVPMaker. Some here have gotten great results using this method.
