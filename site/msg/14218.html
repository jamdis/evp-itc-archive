There are general set of 10 codes that recommended and published for people listing to emergency services radio bands as a go by.  Many departments customize the 10 codes for their department, so from location to location the codes don't always mean the same thing.  When multiple departments are working together Home Land Security recommends that to eliminate possible misunderstandings, 10 codes should not be used.
