A thirty second .wav file costs about 5mb to 6mb, in .mp3 format it is
@ 540kb. Most of my message files, which are about 3 to 5 seconds
(contain 10 to 20 words) are 27kb to 40kb. A huge difference!

I think thats a pretty good measure to go by. If you are posting a
single message file and it is close to 1mb, then its probably too big.
