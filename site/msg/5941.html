Time is absolutely being removed from the audio files. There is so
much more than just listening to the files, you have too look. You
have to train your eyes as well as your ears to make any sort of
judgment. The second I load some of these files, before I listen, I
see problems. I know the site isn't set up to really leave comments on
files with the file but something should be included in the name or
something. Without it, it is not true representation of what the file
is or what has happened between the capture and the post. We listen
and think "Wow! Great catch" but we really do not know. 

Are you starting the scan on your 3 469's at different frequencies?
What is the ratio being mixed? Is it equal between the three? 

I believe that Mark and Debbie Constantino (sp)of Nevada use water
almost exclusively for their recordings. I have found that method to
be difficult in hearing the results. Are you feeling that those
communications are from spirits or earthbounds and what makes you feel
that way?

I've recently started analyzing a 40 min 469 session. The results are
scary. I'd say at this point, and I'm only 1/2 way through, 90% of the
communication is negative in nature and mentions many supposed Demonic
names. The layers of voices are, in many cases, multiple. Often, the
listener hears very little of what is actually said. The voices can be
mocking or downright crude. I have not yet formed an opinion fully but
it would seem to me that these models are easy devices for lower level
entities to figure out how to use. I can not speak of the attitudes or
fears of those investigators present although at times, their voices
are a bit shaky.
