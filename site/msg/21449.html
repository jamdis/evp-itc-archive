Hey Bonnie,

I have always feared the same thing, so i have 2 external drives and an online back up program.

The online one would really benefit you.  If you lose anything, just log in to their site and all your stuff is there.  All you have to do is click recover files and it all goes right back on to your hard drive.

The name of the program is Carbonite, it's $59 a year with unlimited space.

Just "right click" one of your folders and click "back up folder".  That's it, very easy to use.

Here is the web page:

http://www.carbonite.com/

Another option is to back up your stuff onto DVD's.  Depending on how much stuff you have, a blank DVD can hold 4.7gb of space on one disc.
