It is not commonly understood that PK events do not require a local PK agent. While this is the far more common phenomenon, it is by no means the only way in which these events can manifest. I personally have conducted rigorous remote PK experiments many times over the years. This remote intention as it has come to be called is quiet real and remarkably replicable with the right people. This validated phenomena actually explains quite a few cases that are generally attributed to other, sometimes far more bizarre causes.

Of course it makes sense to look for natural causes. However it is appropriate to consider alternatives if you have fail to locate plausible physical causations.
