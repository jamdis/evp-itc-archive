I see two problems right off the bat:

1. As anyone who has been experimenting with these methods can tell
you, not all phrases and statements are heard exactly alike by each
person. That "garbage" as you call it can be a word heard by someone
else that does make since in the grander message.

2. By editing the message and guessing as to what the entity is trying
to say, you are in a since saying you know exactly what they meant in
their communication. It is better to ask for a clearer response to
your question than to mold the answer you get into an answer you want.

No matter how you argue this subject, I always hear more logical
arguments from those against the practice. I'm sure there are many
more points to be made as to why this is a bad idea but these are my
two big ones.
