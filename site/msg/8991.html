Hi Bryan.  It used to be that you could privately contact a person bidding (which I have 
done many times in the past), and give them information if you thought they were being 
taken advantage of.  But due to so many scammers contacting bidders privately, eBay now 
hides the identity of those bidding, and as well, has disabled the ability to send them any 
messages.  

To leave feedback on eBay, you have to have bought something from that person.  
Obviously, if someone has a site, they have control over it and would likely remove any 
type of comments detrimental to their business.  I like the concept though.  :-)

Margaret
