Briefly, Edison appears to have been interested in the problem. No device survives and the drawings that did surface in the late 20th century are suspect according to one of the world's leading Edison experts. At least four accounts are known involving Edison's potential post-mortem contacts through mediums. These mediums are unknown to each other as far as can be determined and the communications range over a period of 60 years.
