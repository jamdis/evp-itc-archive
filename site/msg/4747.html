C'mon Steve--I know I've replied to many of your files, visited your  
site, and spent hours listening to audio.

Yes, it would be nice if more people commented, but sometimes they  
don't because there is nothing to say other than, "I hear it." Which  
raises the question--do we want a thousand posts containing, "I hear  
it"?  If you want specific feedback on a file, let people know what  
you're looking for ("Is this really saying this?", "Is this a man or  
a woman?" "What do you think the last word might be?") I think it  
might encourage meaningful participation.

Oddly enough, people are talking right now because no one is posting  
loads of files, almost certainly because of the holidays. And there  
is nothing wrong with people talking about issues that concern them.  
Sometimes I feel like the list is full of shopping tips for obscure  
paranormal-makeover equipment, but I don't begrudge anyone for  
posting about it.

There are many levels to this list--to any list--which is, at it's  
basis a social network.

HMO and such and if you post a file or two today, I promise I will  
comment. Scout's honor! ;)

Ann
