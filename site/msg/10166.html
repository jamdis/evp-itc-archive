Ron, yes...I say do both.  

I am for whatever works for people in their given situation.  As you 
pointed out, there is value in an intelligent response to a 
question.  Sometimes we need to just let it run and listen, too. 

I try and follow my "gut" with it.  For example, I was at a haunted 
pub, but was skeptical as to the claim of such.  As the night 
progressed, I felt a presence touch me twice.  One place I was "drawn 
to" was the top of some stairs, so I ran a silent box session there - 
just me and whoever wanted to show up - no agenda.  I was able to 
pull out a number of messages after listening to the recording a 
couple times, and one word which I heard live was "raped."  Later, I 
found out that a woman was indeed raped there, and the door I was 
sitting in front of was the former entrance to a brothel.  The woman 
raped and murdered was a prostitute who lost her life up there.  
Follow the gut...there's no rule or law that will work everywhere, 
every time, IMHO.

Regarding EVP, it seems to me that many EVP seem to come near or 
around people talking on the tape.  Do they use the human voices as a 
carrier??
