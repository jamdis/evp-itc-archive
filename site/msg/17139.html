The photographer Fred Picker, when he was still alive and owned Zone VI
Studios, offered equipment of his own manufacture with a lifetime warranty.
It was more expensive than others' similar equipment but was a higher
quality, IMO. Truck run over your Zone VI view camera? He'd replace it, no
questions asked.

I think Calumet Photo still honors the lifetime warranties of the Zone VI
stuff. Hopefully they don't lose too much money on it. I know offering any
sort of support for a product gets expensive.
