{{{ Holly }}}  I'm sorry to read of your frustration and disappointment.  :-(

I don't know what to say except please don't give up.  I believe you when you say you 
would hear something if it was coming through.  Do you have a problem with reception in 
your area?  Have you tried different areas of your home??  I know that just 10 feet can 
make a difference where I live.

Have you tried a manual radio sweep?  I'm pretty sure I'm in the minority among this 
group, but my best results have come from when I am spinning the dial back and forth on 
the radio myself.  I've tried several models, but my favorite so far as been the Duracell 
KP028, though really, almost any radio will do as long as you can comfortably move 
through the channels at a fairly good pace.  LOL - I say 'comfortably' because I have one 
radio with nubs on the thumb dial which has actually given me blisters in the past.

Your mother's name isn't Betty, by chance, is it?  Last week I received a woman's voice (not 
using radio sweep, but foreign crowd babble) saying, "This is Betty."  I also got one last 
year that I think sounds like "Faith for Holly" or "Faith with Holly" or "Faith and Holly."  
Can't quite make out the middle word.  It morphs on me. I hang onto clips like this 
because I never know when I might end up making a connection with the person for whom 
the messages are meant.

Has anyone tried to reach your mom via EVP for you?  I know this isn't the same as getting 
the message yourself, but I would be willing to give it a try and I'm guessing others would 
as well.  Nothing would need to be posted to the boards.  We could just email results, if 
any, to you privately.  Please send me an email if something like this interests you.

Hang in there.  Have a good cry if you need to, but please please please don't give up yet.  

Margaret
