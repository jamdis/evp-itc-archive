No, the echo chamber is not needed, but it does seem to add something, or
allow some other levels access. For the past couple years I've been using
mostly what I call direct mode, or no echo chamber. When I use an aluminum
foil chamber I seem to get more ET types, and that's what I used to get "we
come from the 6th planet" years ago.

 A very large echo chamber can have a dramatic effect, like 12 inches
square, and 4 feet long. I made one that size out of MDF board and lined it
wiht aluminum foil, the voices were so demonic sounding that I took it
apart. Later I found that if something works, you tend to get the negative
guys to try to scare you away from it.
