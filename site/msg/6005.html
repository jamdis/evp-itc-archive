I never said Frank was misguided or selfish but, I'm not surprised you
construed it that way.
Research and researchers in the truest form of the word is and are
open to critique and suggestions. So, no, in the truest meaning I
don't believe so. My comments were absolutely valid and could have
been used in a productive manner but, instead I was deflected and
called names. 
For myself, I believe it is of utmost importance to have an accurate
view of what is really happening. Edited files may indeed be very
accurate but there is no way to tell when the raw is not available and
it causes me great concern that the suggestion was met with such
hostility. If it doesn't cause each of you concern as well, then I
worry about your ability to be objective as well. Have none of you
never wondered why some files here are amazingly clear while others
are far closer to what actually happens with any of the devices? Has
no one else questioned this, even to yourself? I get approximately 2
-3 emails a week from people wondering that. 
You can not become offended and take questions like mine personally. I
was never attacking. I asked for opinions and thoughts about my
questions. 
Do you accept everything you see and hear from everyone at face value?
Do you never have ideas on how things might be improved? Do you never
question? I'm guessing the answer to all three is a "no". 
I never said anyone was faking anything here. If you thought that than
I wonder why you thought that. Personally, I believe that
communication is happening. It is the how and the why and the ways it
can be possibly improved that concern me. It is in knowing what is
being recorded accurately, before it is cut and spliced and amped
otherwise it is not a true representation of what is happening. We
almost completely eliminate the truth in peer review when we only
provide them with an end result not how we got there. 
There is nothing negative in what I posted, only in the reaction. I
have seen Bruce come under fire before but it's these comments and
opinions and suggestions of mine that cause him so much negativity he
wants to leave? 
So, in the end I have a clearer idea. The group is concerned solely
with recording and developing devices to do so. That's fine. Recording
the communications is a good thing. But my opinions thoughts, etc are
not welcome here or desired. That's fine too. 
I wish you all the best in your en devours. Sincerely.

Babs
