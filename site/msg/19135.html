Well I, for one, am glad that you do not drip under pressure Frank (although
I think they have a pill for that)!

You have some interesting stuff posted here and I think that it needs to be
taken into consideration.  There are too many people who are convinced that
everything is a paranormal event.

Like you intimated, most of what we experience is some form of stored
electromagnetic energy.  It builds up and releases in devices like
televisions, refrigerators, light and microwaves.  How often have you heard
your TV make a popping sound when it is off?  We must consider that these
releases of electromagnetic energy travel via waves that can not only
interact  with, but also resonate in wood.  Is it possible that this
frequency resonation can cause a tapping sound once in a while?

I agree fully with the concept of static electricity being responsible for
the willies we get on investigations once in a while.  I am certain that
this is NOT the only cause, but it may play an important role some of the
time.

Show me a picture of an orb and I will show you a picture of a piece of
dust.

But, i am far from a debunking skeptic.  I like to be somewhat rational
before proclaiming that it is a spirit scurrying across the floor in my
attic!  This is why I find GBC so fascinating.  To be able to record the
same voices at different times in different parts of the country is proof
enough for me that there is something fantastic going on with these boxes.
 Whatever is communicating with us is intelligent and responsive, and that
alone should merit further GB research by "science."

This will not happen though.  The implications of discovering life beyond
our own limited 5 senses are too incomprehensible for the ordered world
science has constructed for us.

There are no experts, only experiences.

Tim
