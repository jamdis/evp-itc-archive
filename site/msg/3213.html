Bill, 
I too can be idealistic and am usually dumbfounded by some of the
things that happen within the paranormal community. But, for whatever
reason, I am not idealistic when it comes to the possibilities of the
distribution of the box to whoever has the money to pay for one.
IMO, this reeks of money over-riding responsibility. This is a huge
step-forward in our field and I applaud the research and abilities
being shown. 
Like absolutely attracts like. And, as we all know, egos abound in
this field. The energies present when the box is turned on has a huge
effect on who comes through. I know this to be true.
Now, I don't have a proposed answer to my dilemma. I do not know how
to regulate the people who purchase. The only answer I can come up
with is time. Time to fully research the possibilities. The possible
psychological effect on untrained users, scares me. And although I get
that the onus of responsibility ultimately falls on the owner of the
box, I can't help but feel the responsibility falls on all of us. True
professionalism, I believe, would have the builders and distributers
testing the units over a period of time, at the very least. I'm pretty
sure Frank didn't call everyone on day one.
