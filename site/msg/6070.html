> If you already have your RS box and it is modified, you are 75% of the way
> there.
> I will explain in detail how to do a successful session.
> Let your spirit friends know you are about to turn the box on and ask if
> they would please join you in a communication session.
> Turn on your recording device(whichever you choose to record the session)
> state the date and time and number the session so you have a referral at a
> later date.
> Turn your RS box on and start the scan. I prefer and always use the AM
> band to do a session, it is less cluttered than FM and I find it easier to
> hear real time messages and answers.
> At first you can ask simple questions that will require short answers such
> as " are there any spirits listening that wish to communicate with me"
> "can you say my name", " can you tell me your name" questions along those
> line will glean short understandable answers and messages. Not only for
> hearing in real time but also later when reviewing the recording.
>
> It will take time for you to develop what we call an ear for hearing the
> communication, that just means your listening skills during a session will
> become more honed and acute to the messages and answers being sent. Do not
> get discouraged if you do not hear any responses the first couple of
> sessions. Our friends need time to adjust to a new researcher and assign
> you a technician.
> But you can rest assured the communication will come.
>
> Keep the sessions to a limit of 10 to 15 minutes as to not overwhelm
> yourself when you need to listen back carefully to the recording or stress
> the energy supply of your spirit contacts.
>
> When you are done with your session let your spirit friends know you are
> about to end the session and turn the box off and give them about ten
> seconds before you do turn the box off.
>
> extend the same courtesy as you would speaking to someone on the phone,
> say goodbye and thank you and you will almost always get a cordial
> response.
>
> shut your box and again thank them for their efforts. you have now done a
> rudimentary box session. load it onto the computer and review it for
> messages and answers to your question that you missed during the live
> session. when you have done this post a message to the board and I will
> explain how to do a basic edit of the recording to better understand the
> responses that are less clear.
>
> good luck Carol and I am looking forward to researching with you.
