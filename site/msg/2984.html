I do not know for sure exactly who is communicating with us via Frank's 
boxes or what their intents are. This is a new mystery opened up by our 
newer technology. It interested me that they make predictions that don't 
happen, or in some cases seem to be lying.

About 10-15 years ago quite a few people (myself included) built what we 
call "Barkhausen effect detectors". You can see the details at the 
website below.  The Barkhausen detector starts about 1/3 of the way down 
the page and goes to the bottom. A few of the parts are VERY difficult to 
come by. The inventor sent me a couple extra pieces of his material used 
to make the coil core.

http://amasci.com/freenrg/bark.html

Most of us picked up voices on these things at one time or another.  Alas, 
neither I nor anyone else recorded them. We had no idea where the voices 
were coming from.  Some people felt they were extraterrestrials.  It 
seemed odd that they spoke in Earth languages and usually the language of 
the user, though.

I still have the device around somewhere and might dig it out one of 
these days (I think it's in the attic) and do some more experimentation 
with it.
