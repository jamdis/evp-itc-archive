Misty has already left for work so if you don't mind I will tell you what the water method is, as she is the one who taught me how to do it. It is very simple.... I don't talk during the recording, instead I ask my questions or make any comments before I record.  I then record anywhere from 30 seconds to 2 minutes, anything over that and you will risk fatiguing your ear trying to hear the voices.

Then with Audacity or some other sound editing program she will change the speed by slowing the recording down by 74%. Of course this works for her ear and not mine. I use her second filter and do not slow it down at all.

Whenever I use this method I get very good class A voices. If you wanna try it let her know and she will be happy to send you the filters.
