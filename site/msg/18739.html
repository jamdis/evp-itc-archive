I use an Olympus VN960PC. It's a nice recorder but they have come out with
advanced models for about the same cost as mine. I think they have models in
the same series that record 24 hours or more of audio in the Hi-Q position.
BTW I would only use the Hi-Q position with this machine unless I'm just
recording notes to myself.

The things I like about it are that it records each new audio in a new file,
it has three general use folders and one that goes by date, has lots of
features and it's very easy to use. Batteries last a long time.

Things I don't like about this particular model are that it only has 6 hours
of recording time, and you have to use their special program to download the
files to your computer, and it takes a long time. 6 hours of audio takes
about 20 minutes to download.

Zack
