I've been reverting my recording chain to all analog devices. Linear FM sweep audio is fed to an LED, this light from the LED is captured by a vintage Phototube. The signal from the phototube is recorded directly to a VCR audio input. VCR's make awesome low noise analog tape decks. Some distortion is introduced converting the analog tape to mp3 for the computer.
