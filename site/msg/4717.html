It's farily straight forward to repair, you can tell what conductor 
goes to what conductor on the board. It's just a matter of making 
little jumpers with small wire to bridge the breaks. Radio shack has 
#30 wire wrap wire that works well for that kind of stuff. Maybe 
there's enough of the original pad left on the connectors that you 
could glue them back in place, then jumper the breaks. I could look 
at it tomorrow, as well.
