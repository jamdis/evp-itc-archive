I used some items I had laying around my basement.
The noise generation item is my ole favorite, the 'My Lil Reminder' 
20 second digital recorder/player. The same one I use in the Mike's 
Box. I had a couple extras so I used it after recording some white 
noise to its chip.
The microphone was one from a wear-on-the-head boom microphone.  I 
took it apart and attached it inside the black box.
I then placed some cotton over the top of it to help filter out the 
louder audio of the white noise.
The box has a small hole in it cover with a rubber piece, so a bit of 
audio can come through from outside and be capture by the mic.

With the 8 foot cable, I can add and extension to that in case you 
want to lower it into a well or something like that.  The box will 
keep the microphone shelterd in a damp environment.
