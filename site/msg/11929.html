"Matrixing" occurs when a signal is right in the noise level (or below 
it). When the signal to noise ratio is greater, it's no longer matrixing 
and most people can hear/see the same thing without any hints.

Sometimes processing/filtering of signals buried in the noise (done 
simply with various programs on pictures, audio recordings, RF etc. 
without significantly altering the content) will reveal there is nothing 
or something there.
