Hi,

When it comes to EVP, I was one of the most cynical/skeptical people ever.  Well, I was until about two weeks ago.

Then, a box cut me off in the middle of a rhetorical question. It was speaking clearly -- and directly to me -- from across the room. That got my attention in a hurry.

Electronics is not my field, so I won't pretend to understand how the box works, but I'm pretty darned sure it's not all in my head.

Though I was a very busy speaker that weekend, I kept trying to be around the box during our ghost investigations, etc., just to hear more.  

It would be so much simpler if I could shrug this off and say, "It's probably just my imagination."

Instead, I want to know more about the voices, what they're saying, why they're saying what they do, and... well, what they are.  

To me, this is fascinating because of the potential variables.

But, this isn't convenient and it wasn't what I had in mind when I packed my bag to speak in Canada.  Right now, I should be finishing three books I'm writing, one of which is very overdue.

Instead, I'm sitting here, wide-eyed, listening to recordings posted to this Yahoo Group, and I'm trying to put this into a context that I can easily compartmentalize.

So far, that's not working.  I'm finding no easy answers.

Oh, I'm grateful to each person who's posting their recordings. Thank you!  But each recording makes me even more sure that I haven't a clue what this is... and that's a challenge that's singing a siren song. 

Cheerfully,
Fiona
