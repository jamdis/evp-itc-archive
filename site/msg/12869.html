Hello Frank and All,
 
Dave and his wife Sharon are founders of the International Ghost Hunters Society.  They make a lot of money selling equipment, teaching and certifying people who are willing to pay the enormous prices they charge. They have also been around for quite a while.
 
I respect that they are serious researchers, however their newsbulletins (which I subscribe to out of curiosity) hit upon three points in every issue.
 
1.  We went here and there and this is how great we are.
 
2.  Everyone else sucks.
 
3.  Buy our products and pay outrageous prices if you want to experience the paranormal the 'right' way.
 
Other than that you're not missing anything. The 'Digcom' is overpriced. If you need to buy it because you can't make it yourself here then it is cheaper to buy it on ebay. 
 
Wish I had something a little more posititve to say about Dave and Sharon. I have an associate who has gone on investigations with them and says they are good people.  I remind myself of that when reading their bulletins that rip others and checking out the prices they are charging.
 
I guess it is appropriate to add that in my group, two are certified parapsychologists and we teach paranormal studies in our area for FREE.  I also distribute to my 150 plus online members (FREE membership) FREE downloads (by permission of course) of Sarah Estep's 'Voices of Eternity' as well as the FREEware sound editing program Audacity with instructions for use authored by Shannon McCabe of HPI and FREE downloads of paranormal paperwork (such as investigation interviewing questionnaires, permission and safety forms, etc...) because I believe in bringing this experience with EVERYONE, not just those with extra money, because who is that anyway(?). Not me for sure.
 
Anyway, I wish all (whether solid or ethereal) a safe and happy weekend. 
 
~Clara
