OK - I just received my used 12-470 older version radio today and 
hacked it in about 10 minutes.  Works great, BUT I noticed on mine 
the length of the battery wires were not as generous as on Mike's 
video or the 12-469 I hacked previously.  Thus, I had to sort of keep 
it almost together, peering deep inside with a magnifying glass to 
make sure I had the correct wire (7th from top - green for this one) 
and somehow snip it.  

I guess I am reporting this, because you really have to pay attention 
to the length of the battery box wires when you tear into these - 
they do vary, and are easy to accidentally detach off of the board.
