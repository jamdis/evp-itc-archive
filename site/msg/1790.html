Hi Steve and Bruce,

I would like to post my theory concerning Bruces manual sweep method.
The receiver he is using is likely to have a built in automatic
frequency control circuit. This is a sort of feedback voltage that tends
to hang on to a tuned station while tuning past in the FM band.

If a relatively slow manual sweep is used, one would tend to hear whole
words or even phrases from strong stations in the band. This is the same
effect one gets with the automatic scan tuning set for a slow scan rate.
The receiver dwells on a station longer and captures more of the vocal
segment being broadcast.

I, however, can't say if any one method is better than another...except
for the resulting massage clarity and content received. This is judged
by the user!

Joe Cioppi
