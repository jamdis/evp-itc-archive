Ann, your post about what happened with INIT is so relevant to what
still happens today - even in this forum. Personality conflicts, egos,
aggression, all the not so nice aspects of human behavior really
affect what happens in this field. Although I have two of Marks books
I don't have Miracles but it sounds like it has some important lessons.

Everyone has a belief system that affects their opinions of this
research, but if people can't get past the petty conflicts then we
can't even continue collaborating to advance the connections we're
trying to establish that may exist outside the physical world. I am
grateful to most of the people in this forum but must admit I'm glad
there was some recent housekeeping as it made it difficult to want to
work through all the post. 

Regarding Mark's work: for me it compliments the Box research. I've
had some amazing solo journeys using nothing but a meditative mind and
Mark's Monroe Institute cd but the Box  seems to bring experimental
communication research into a group environment that I haven't found
elsewhere.  -Bill
