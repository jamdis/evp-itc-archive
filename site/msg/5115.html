Just to give my take on the topic.  Bruce has done box research 
almost as long as i have and has had similar results with various 
boxes.  However, i tend to leave the box run on occasion to help with 
the communication.  Sometimes i get blocked or they are struggling 
for some reason to come through and at that point i leave it run.  I 
let them know that i am leaving the box running and to work there way 
through to find the right frequency again.  They usually say ok and i 
walk away and after that the next session usually produces better 
results.  I really don't think it's disrespectful to do that, i just 
feel that leaving it on helps them work on their communication skills 
and seems to work for me.  I do also use the "Go back" theory before 
i shut it off, that theory dates back to a time i seemed to have 
trouble with noises and stuff in my house and it was box related.  
Box or any other device i believe opens a portal and needs to be 
closed on completion of the session.  Although, they are always 
around and really could come and go as they please. But using these 
boxes is like a great big "COME ON IN" sign and you need to use 
common sense.
