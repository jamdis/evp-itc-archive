It's a box that you can ony see at night! 


 Just kidding!

 It's a swept radio with amplifiers to drive a speaker with the radio 
audio, there's mic amps to pick up the resulting audio, usually from 
a sound chamber, then provide a line level output to a recorder, and 
monitor speaker. 

 It's a method of  supplying raw audio that spirits/entites can use 
to form their voices, resulting in much louder EVP type recordings. 
and consistent spirit contacts. it's possible to get two way 
communication, and question/answers, although I rarely ask questions.

 It was kind of an analog version of EVPmaker. After I had many 
messages relayed from spirits that couldn't use the computer, I was 
thinking about what else I could use and came up with the ghost box.
