I tried an Ouija board once. My brother and I were given one by my
grandmother, who found it in her attic. After asking many questions and
getting all sorts of answers that ranged from gibberish to the totally
absurd, I think we decided there was something in our joint subconsciouses
that was making the planchette give the answers it did.

That may not always be the case, though. I think if answers of some sort
were given involving something that no one present would have the faintest
idea about, and they turned out to be true or valid, then it might
demonstrate spirit communication.

Zack
