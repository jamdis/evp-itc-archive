The only conclusion I have so far is that providing a raw material for the
entities to form their voices from seems to make the communication possible.
Any raw audio will work, but it seems to make it easier if there's bits of
speech present. EVPmaker provides those bits of speech, and sweeping a radio
across it's tuning range is a convenient source of speech bits. You can also
use crown noise, fan noise, running water noise, wind noise--any noise that
has sound that the human ear can perceive. You can use a foreign language
you don't understand as the raw audio.

 Yeah, radio by itself, as in just a carrier wave would do nothing for
spirit communication, it's only when the radio signal is demodulated in a
receiver to provide the bits of speech music and noise that it becomes
useful in spirit communication. Just one method of many to provide the raw
audio.
