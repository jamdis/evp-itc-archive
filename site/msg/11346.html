I remember that someone had selled a "raudive diode" build into a microphone plug, which is very similar to a crystal radio. It shouldn't be difficult to solder a diode, 2 resistors and 2 capacitors into a plug and test it. The schematic is here: http://www.worlditc.org/c_04_s_bridge_10.htm
