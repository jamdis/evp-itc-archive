This is the charger circuit I'm using, and is an example of the stuff I go 
out on the web for. In #21, I used just a constant voltage regulator for the 
charger, which works ok for the smaller batteries. This one uses the same 
regulators with current limiting. The charge current is supposed to go down 
to "float" level after batteries are charged to maintain the charge, so you 
don't have to worry about disconnecting the charger.
