Hi Jim.  I had some success using DC6 Live Forensics and real-time monitoring with 
headphones, using no background at all, as well as using radio sweep.  But as you 
mentioned, the filters that worked well one session often did not work the next.  Go 
figure! ;-)

The demo of version 6 Forensics was cool because it worked for only 1 minute at a time 
but never expired.  The demo of version 7 Forensics is a full working version, without time 
restrictions, but after 30 days, it no longer works.  

I used the setup a little differently in that I didn't use DC to record my sessions.  I used a 
second computer and a splitter line for the sound so when the 1 minute time limit hit, I 
didn't have to stop the session, just rehit the play button so there was only a second or so 
gap between each minute.  As I recall my setup looked like this:

External mic to PC sound in
Split line from PC sound out going to A) Mac laptop sound in & B) my headphones

Looking back through my notes, I had some pretty good results with the following 
settings:

Continuous Noise First
* Attack 10
* Release 364
* Attenuation 31
* CNF Forensic
* FTF 2048
* Overlap 50%

Brick Wall Second
* Frequency 3
* Upper Frequency 8291
* Length 948

I just tried a variety of settings willy-nilly and happened to have success with this one 
among a couple others.  

Margaret
