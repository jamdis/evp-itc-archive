It doesn't matter, the radio sweeps by itself anyway.  Both versions 
i believe have buttons on it with a digital face.  Mine is White, 
i'm not sure if that's the old design or new.  The White one you 
have to pull the pin off the board, it's 3rd up from the bottom and 
says mute next to it.  I used a pair of needle noise pliers and 
ripped it out, but probably not the best method.  You probably 
should heat up the solder on the back of the board and than just 
pull it out.  It works really well though, i'm gonna try $1 store 
radios next and see what happens.  If it works they also have $1 
store speakers too.  A nice $2 combo box w/speakers, sounds too good 
to be true so i'm sure you can't modify the cheap ones.

-Steve H.
