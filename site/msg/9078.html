Hi Michael.  I have had the honor many times of either receiving messages for someone 
from their loved one and passing them on (to people I know) or in helping people learn to 
record for and receive their own messages.  Along with research in trying to improve 
communication with the other side, this is my primary work with EVP... helping others who 
are grieving make contact with their loved ones.  I do not charge for this.

I think to charge, or attempt to charge, anyone for a service (spiritual or otherwise) on the 
backend is slimy.  However, if there is an agreement in advance about fees and services, 
then that is up to the consenting parties.  People in all types of fields get paid for their 
time, regardless of the service being offered.  If the fee is clear, the service being offered 
legitimate, so be it.  As long as people aren't being scammed.  grrrr...... I don't like to see 
people taken advantage of in any field of service (as one crooked auto mechanic tried to 
do recently with my mother.)  Double grrrrrr........  
 
As for figuring out who a message belongs to?  I think we have to be very cautious in 
coming forward with content in recordings that were not requested by the living loved one 
of the person in spirit.  It's sort of like the doctor's creed to first do no harm.  I feel unless 
you know someone well enough to be fairly certain that your doing so would not 
traumatize them, you have to err on the side of caution and not say anything. 

For instance, there are some people who, due to their religious beliefs, could feel that you 
are being fooled by a demon or some other evil and that your saying you are in a touch 
with a loved one of theirs is not only not true, but cruel of you to claim so.  Even worse, 
would be those who actually do believe you have heard from their loved one, but take that 
to mean they are not in 'Heaven' (as some believe those in Heaven do not have the ability 
to communicate here.)  There's all kinds of beliefs which differ from our reality that our 
loved ones can be exactly where they are supposed to be, in a good place, and still talk to 
us via EVP.  We have to make sure that there is a similarity in world views.

In regard to mediumship:  Even as a experienced EVPer, and having made connections with 
my own loved ones, I still benefited from my sitting for an hour with a medium.  One of 
the really neat things is, I brought my recorders and upon playback, can actually hear EVP 
that relates to what the medium is saying.  So, as you wrote, that was icing on the cake.  
But the sitting alone, even without the EVP heard later, provided an explanation for events 
and a peace of mind to me about something I had carried with me for 25yrs and had not 
been able to resolve via EVP alone.  The fee for that sitting was some of the best money 
I've ever spent in my life, as what I got from it was priceless.

Personally, I'd like to see some joint studies done with mediums and EVP, each modality 
helping to validate the other in the process.   :-)

Margaret
