I use a 12-469, although I just bought a 12-587 because I'm so tired of the clicking; I'm not sure if it's going to work as good or not, as I just modified it last night and haven't had a chance to use it yet.  Currently I have the 12-469 plugged into a Tascam Porta05 ministudio 4-track tape recorder, along with a Sure microphone.  So far I've found doing sessions during the daylight hours on clear days seems to work the best; an entity once told me to do the sessions at night, but it doesn't seem to work very well then -- at least, if they talk then, I can't hear it for all the noise.  Although I'm a newbie, my one piece of advice for you would be to listen back to the tape or digital recorder files VERY closely, because for me, 90% of the messages can only be heard upon review, not during the actual session itself (maybe because some of it is true EVP?).

I would say, if you can get your hands on a small radio to hack, you will have no problem getting something to happen.
