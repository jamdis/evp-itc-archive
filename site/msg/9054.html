Hi Laura.  The current pricing is as follows:

Basic USA Version $399 - Includes Shipping and Insurance
Crystal Enhanced Version  $449 - Includes Shipping and Insurance
The pricing for the soon-to-come advanced MaxiBox has not yet been released.

I think the MiniBox is very different from shack hacks.  I find that I get much more 
technical chatter on it, and not so much from loved ones.  Though there is a group from 
The Big Circle which particularly likes to come through on it.  I prefer the MiniBox results 
second to manual radio sweep, but over the hacked radio shack units.

Of course, everybody will have various opinions about the multitude of techniques and 
models out there, depending on their preferences and the results they get with each 
method.

Given that both manual radio sweep and inexpensive automated sweep units provide fairly 
good results for most people at a far more budget-friendly price, it's hard to say whether 
or not the MiniBox is worth the price.  

A couple features the MiniBox offers, that are not available for use on the regular or 
hacked units is, the ability to control both the sensitivity level of the antenna, and the rate 
at which the broadcast spectrum is swept.  This allows for fine tuning, so to speak.

If you go to the paranormalsystems.com web site, there is a video you can watch, showing 
one of the early MiniBoxes and it's features.

Margaret
