Today was the first run of the brown noise session, I ran for about 10
minutes, and upon review, there were some short contacts, one named
Mike interestingly advised "empty" when I asked what was in my left
hand (he was correct, the item was in my right hand, I simply
misspoke). Today's session was abbreviated, I had poor reception for
some reason, the .469 was doing great, but the .120 was only receiving
strongly in FM. On the surface I would say it was a success, I will
have to correlate more sessions with white and pink noise loops to
determine. 

On a more personal note, I find brown/red noise a heckuva lot easier
on the ears since most of the amplitude of the signal is at lower
frequency. Reminds me of a large, fast river as heard from a bank.
