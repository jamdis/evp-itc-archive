Hi Ron,

I don't think that it's possible to *not* get EVP. However, it needs
some practice to recognize them; one has to get used to the specific
sound, rhythm and grammar of EVP. Perhaps it is better for newbies to
start with the basic recording techniques to learn hearing EVP and
distinguish between what real EVP could be, and what probably not, i.e.
use only recorder + microphone, and later then perhaps natural or
artificial background sounds like water or radio as a "raw material".

As to your EVPmaker recording, in the original file there aren't any
beeps. You should perhaps re-download the file:
http://tech.groups.yahoo.com/group/EVP-ITC/files/Stefan/SJ-Allophones.wav
And also do use EVPmaker's Cue List option, since otherwise EVPmaker
doesn't play the individual allophones but only chopped fragments from
the file.
