We have been using the box for several weeks now and have had some startling results.  Our result do in fact seem to be in direct correlation with the case we're working on.  I have also experienced words coming from the box, that seem to be repeating people in the room.  The first time I used the box my eleven year old son burst into the room to asked what we were having for dinner.  I replied "There's roast beef in the oven", the box said "beef", very clear and distinct.  On another level, while recording the sessions, we were able to pick up full sentences with many voices all speaking at once.  These were captured the same way you would capture voices during EVP sessions using a digital recorder.  If anyone would like to hear samples of these I will be more than happy to send a few to you.  I prefer not to post them on esnips at this time as they are from a case I am currently working on.

Frank, what you are doing with the boxes is interesting. I think the draw to the box for ghost hunters is that it "appears" to be a communication device with the dead. I've been experimenting a lot with the radio shack hack box and I have been getting a lot. For the most part I am using it to get answers to questions I or others have in areas that have had strange events or just a paranormal nature to them. There is definitely communication at a rudimentary level although I doubt that all of it is honest communication. I definitely see a lot of the trickster influence with these boxes. It can and does tell people what they want to hear, and it seems to be reading the minds of the participants. Like someone will crack a joke and the box will respond 2 seconds later saying simply "joke". I was using the box in one area with a recently married woman and the word pregnant came out of the box 3 times during the 2 sessions. I think the box was picking up on her conscious or subconscious thoughts. I have not gotten the word pregnant since then.
 
So far both radio shack types I have are working spectacularly for me. One of the more interesting responses I've gotten out of the box is that it has said "ovulous" backwards (it said "75" forwards), and that was just 10 minutes or so after I was playing around with the Ovilus. I don't know exactly how The Ovilus is supposed to be pronounced but the box pronounced it like I spelled it above.
 
So what is going on with these boxes is extremely interesting and not readily explainable by science, but I lean away from thinking the communications are coming from ghosts or demons, I am thinking it is just transferring thought into messages or it is higher beings answering our questions (although not always truthfully). I have to say I was highly skeptical that I would get anything with these type of boxes but I have been fascinated by the results. I've played what I am hearing for others and have done sessions with others, and these people have all been impressed too. Thanks for bringing this technology to the forefront like you have.
