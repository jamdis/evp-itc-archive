Rita, I think you will have to get a pair of amplified speakers on 
that 469, and put them in a sound chamber of some sort.  In that 
chamber, you will want probably an amplified microphone running into 
your voice recorder, to pick up the sounds bouncing around in there.  
You can also add some white noise into the box, but I think people 
are getting results without adding the additional noise. The chamber 
seems to really bounce the sound around and give you a good mix to 
record.
