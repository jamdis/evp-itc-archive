Hello, Kristin...

I guess when I think about "portal," it really comes down to 
what "their" definition of the word is versus "my" definition.  

The word "portal" just means "doorway."  Are these ITC 
devices "doorways" between both worlds for communication?  Well, yes, 
in the sense that they serve as a "bridge" between worlds for 
communication purposes.  However, I truly struggle to think that 
spirits/ghosts can use this device to physically manifest in one's 
home.  Heck, if we're hearing them, they are already around us, 
IMHO.  This is why when these devices goes to a highly active 
location, we record a bunch of stuff...they are already there. 

Now, I will say that I have been noticing that after doing spirit 
rescue with these devices, it seems more lost souls seem to be 
brought for help.  We recently assisted two ghosts at an apartment, 
and believed the location to be cleared.  A week later, the owner 
records a female EVP asking, "Help me," and little attention getters 
started happening again (lightbulbs "popping", TVs turning on by 
themselves, etc)  None of the sensitives/psychics or the spirit box 
detected another spirit in need of help prior. I keep thinking that 
benevolant spirits when they know we can help, will make desperate 
attempts to bring lost earthbound spirits for assistance. ;)
