Thanks for listening and for the feedback, Stefan & Rees.  

Stefan, just a quick clarification ... I have removed the 'junk' between my voice and the 
answer.  If I'm going to get an answer, it's usually between .5-7 or so seconds, and I 
usually note that in my description as I don't want to mislead people about the timing of 
the response.  Still, I guess that can be considered a fairly immediate answer.  :0)

Many times the program has run for about a minute without creating any clear phrases (I 
keep each session under 2 minutes), relevant or otherwise.  I really like this fact, because 
in my opinion, it greatly reduces the chances of a false positive.  

Thank you so much for sharing your creativity and giving us researchers additional tools 
to experiment with!  This has been really fun and I plan to keep exploring the potential of 
your software and the allophones file.

Margaret
