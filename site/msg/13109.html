Well I was simplifying but most broadcast antennas are setup for very little radiation directly below as well as above as it is a waste of power.  AM *loves* to shoot straight up because of the bounce (as you HAM guys know) but TV and FM wants as much power as possible to be at the top for the farthest line of sight broadcast.

I was amazed to read that you guys got FM chatter on the recorder.  I guess it is possible in theory but I would never had expected that.  Just goes to show why it is all radio theory and not radio proof or law :))  Those EM fields are tricky little buggers.
