"Hello all,

Sarah Estep, the founder of the AA-EVP and an important EVP pioneer,

 The funeral service will be held at Kalis Funeral Home, 2973 Solomons
 Island Road in Edgewater, Maryland; on Tuesday Jan 8, 2008 at 10 a.m.

If you personally knew Sarah or if she influenced you and you would
