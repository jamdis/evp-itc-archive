This same spirit once shouted me out while I was in my daily meditation, so out loud that I thought she was a neighbour, then I could hear also her steps outside the room, then I began to realize that the steps never fade out, I didn't panick cause I thought it was my neighbour, but when I realized that it was not, immediately my guide appeared to me in a light form, and told me (telepathically) not to be afraid, so I did that, then ignored all the thing, this was almost 4 years ago,  but I think these communications are from the same entity, its a woman, I don't think that if I talk to her, she would change her ill- humor, what about if she wants attention just to get in and then I wouldn't be able to put her out?

I can't understand why a spirit can be so envious, when she shouted me while in meditation, she said "YOU SHUT UP NOW"  very strongly and in a very bad humor. In the EVPs I ignored her, but I think she wants to catch some attention, but I can't trust her, even if she change her attitude. Spirits can be sometimes very tricky.
 
Intuition can help us very much in these cases, but not always.
