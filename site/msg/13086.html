Tom,
 
I own a Motorola Razor. On a recent investigation I had my Razor set to vibrate. I recieved a phone call that set off the EMF detector and it subsequently "spiked" from the color green to red and did so for each vibra signal the the phone was receiving... This false positive reading led me to make sure that everyones phone was instead turned off entirely.
 
I have also recieved radio interference or bleed over from other two-way radios in the area that can be confused as EVP if not mentioned when recording audio. Still, I find 2-way radios convenient if not necessary for larger scale field investigations. I'm sure my colleagues in the Gettysburg area would agree with me ... but I also hear from my friend Bill Murphy that many have resorted to hand held GPS systems to mark particular areas of interest when conducting EVP or ITC sessions in the field...
