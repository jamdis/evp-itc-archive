This is my two cents regarding the box and the "spread" of it.

I would think the goal would be to get people experimenting with it
as much as possible. There are always going to be people who shoot
it down, attack, etc., but you will find others who experiment with
it, or embrace it, as well. Nothing that is new (or old) will ever
be exempt from the attack of others, unless of course one keeps it
hidden in a closet.

With this type of ITC, it is ground-breaking IMHO, as the box is
providing an avenue to "open up the minds" of those whose time has
come to "hear." In other words, I suspect that only a given amount
of people will be open to it; and that will be their time to explore
the boxes and make contact with spirit. That is the goal I would
think: more belief by the populus and especially connection to the
unseen, whatever that may be.

I just received a nasty e-mail from a TAPS lover attacking Angels &
Ghosts because I have written some articles with respect to their
shoddy review of the Shadow Man pic at Moundsville (I am not dogging
TAPS here). I simply felt what was done by the TV show to the lady
who took the image was wrong. I also was attacked as to some of the
EVPs on Angels & Ghosts as being "faked." We receive submissions
from all over the world and post those that are of interest (photos,
videos, evp, stories, etc). The only goal of the site is to post
that which might cause others to consider the existence of spirit -
the existence of more beyond the physical realm. Of course, we don't
try to post that which we know was faked. Again pointing others to
connect spiritually is the idea.

Is that not what the boxes will be first used for? I could see many
people split on this, but the idea of turning more toward the
spiritual (whatever your definition of that is), would be a basic
goal. From there, everyone must have their own personal experience
to become a believer, no matter how good the evidence posted from
anyone experimenting with ITC.
