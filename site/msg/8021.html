William,

I believe the water method is used with traditional EVP and can not 
be used for box communication. I believe it is when a researcher 
turns on a faucet or some other type of running water source and 
proceeds to record the way you would record any traditional evp 
session. I think the premise is that any spirits willing or wishing 
to communicate use the white noise sound of the running water to form 
the words they place on the recording medium.

I personally have never heard any files or recordings using this 
method that contained any spirit communication. I have a fish tank in 
my bedroom that is continually making the sound of running water.
At night when it is very quiet and I am relaxed by the sound the 
water makes, I could swear I can hear voices within the sound of the 
water.

I do not rule out any possibility but in my experience it is most 
likely your mind manipulating the sound and trying to make sense of 
it.

Bruce
