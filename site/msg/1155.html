Last night I made an interesting discovery. I placed a suction cup induction coil (Radio Shack) on top of a package of small rare earth magnets and found the package became a very sensitve contact microphone. I tested placing this combo on several objects around the house as a test. The most sensitve object was my old Presto quartz heater. I set the combo on top of the heater and have recorded a few sessions that had good results. Best results were using AM radio sweep in the background. Here is an example and photo's of the set-up. The induction mic gets plugged into a pre-amp (not shown).
