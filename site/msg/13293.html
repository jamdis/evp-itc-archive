It depends on how you present it to the children. My children are well aware of my activities, though I don't allow them to partake (execpt for the building of the equipment). Then again, they haven't really shown any interest except for asking if ghosts are bad or good. In that case I tell them they are like people, some are bad and some are good.
 
Some people believe in shielding children from this stuff, I say it depends on the child. If you have a child who is easily scared or influenced, I would not expose them to ITC. Being that my kids are more interested in the technology than the contacts, I am not really worried about their mental health. Then again, my children have been raised with the understanding that these things are real, unseen things exist and what you feel and think concerning these things really matter.
