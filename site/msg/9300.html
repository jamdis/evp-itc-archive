I know what you mean about the excitement. I've spent a lot of long nights 
working on something and not even realizing what time it was!

The good thing about my pcb making is that I can make as many or as few 
as I want. I've tried making them in batches, where I make maybe 4 at a 
time, and depending on the size of the board it can take the same amount

the bugs worked out, these might be a good source of power.
